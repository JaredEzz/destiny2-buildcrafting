## Exotic — Getaway Artist
Consume your Arc grenade (held input) to summon an enhanced Arc Soul that fires faster, lasts longer, and makes you Amplified. This gives you a THIRD permanent buddy alongside the Bleak Watcher turret and Hellion. Critically in Monument of Triumph (Update 9.7.0), Arc Soul, Hellion, and the Bleak Watcher turret all count as grenade-derived companions, so they inherit the damage bonus from your Grenade stat — at ~200 Grenade your buddies gain roughly a 65% damage increase, turning passive add-clear into real DPS.

## Abilities
- **Super:** Needlestorm (Strand) — best add-clear + boss DPS pairing for a grenade/buddy build; alternatively Song of Flame if you want to convert grenades into ability-spam during your Transcendence/Super window. Needlestorm is the default endgame pick because the Threadling needles benefit from your massive Grenade stat investment.
- **Class Ability:** Healing Rift
- **Grenade:** Storm Grenade (Arc) — the only Arc grenade on Prismatic Warlock, and Getaway Artist requires an Arc grenade. Holding the grenade input consumes it, which spawns the enhanced Arc Soul AND a Bleak Watcher Stasis turret from the same consume; tapping instead throws a normal Storm Grenade (which Jolts via Facet of Dominance).
- **Melee:** Arcane Needle (Strand) — three tracking charges that Unravel targets; pair with Facet of Balance for melee energy from Light kills. (Some run the Stasis Penumbral Blast for extra freeze synergy with Bleak Watcher; Arcane Needle is the higher-DPS default.)

### Aspects
- Bleak Watcher — converts your grenade into a Stasis turret that slows and freezes targets (counts as a grenade source, so it scales with your Grenade stat)
- Hellion — casting your class ability summons a Solar Hellion buddy that bombards enemies (also scales with the Grenade stat under 9.7.0)

### Fragments
- Facet of Courage — your Light abilities (Arc Soul, Hellion) deal +10% damage to targets afflicted by Darkness debuffs (huge with frozen/slowed targets from Bleak Watcher)
- Facet of Balance — grenade energy on Darkness kills, melee energy on Light kills; keeps the turret/grenade loop fed
- Facet of Awakening — defeating groups with matching damage spawns elemental pickups (Light/Dark) to fuel Transcendence
- Facet of Dominance — your Arc grenades Jolt (and Void grenades Weaken); tapped/thrown Storm Grenades let your turret-frozen targets also take chain damage and feed the Jolt loop with Encrypted Data Disk

(Hellion and Bleak Watcher each grant 2 fragment slots, so the build caps at 4 Facets.)

## Weapons
- Exotic Primary: Graviton Lance or Sunshot for ad-clear chain explosions while your buddies work — frees you to play the orb/Transcendence economy
- Exotic alt: Microcosm or a Strand/Arc trace for boss DPS during your damage window
- Legendary Stasis or Strand primary (Adaptive/Precision frame) — note: champion stunning is now intrinsic to the weapon frame in 9.7.0, so just bring frames that cover the GM's champion types
- Special: a strong Rocket-Assisted Sidearm or Wave-frame GL (intrinsic Unstoppable) for champion coverage + chunk damage — or a Sniper Rifle if you want the ranged single-target option that feeds the Reload at Range / Sniper's Meditation artifact perks
- Heavy: Heavy GL (e.g., a Demolitionist/Bait-and-Switch roll), a high-frame rocket/LMG, or a Linear Fusion Rifle for boss DPS that complements buddy chip damage

## Stat Priority
Grenade (push to 200 — entire build scales here, buddies get up to ~65% damage at 200) > Class (100+ for the overshield-on-rift tier and faster Hellion/rift regen) > Health (100 for survivability layer) > Weapons > Super > Melee. The whole build is a "stack Grenade as high as possible" build; everything else is survivability and uptime.

## Artifact — Encrypted Data Disk *(re-evaluated)*

This build is fundamentally an Arc-grenade-Jolt + freeze + orb/ability-regen engine, not an element-match exercise. Encrypted Data Disk is the only artifact whose perk density lines up with the actual loop: the thrown Storm Grenade Jolts via Facet of Dominance, and EDD turns those Jolted kills into the build's economy. Dielectric (Arc-debuff kills -> Bolt Charge + Orb + heal), Blinding Jolts (final blows on Jolted targets blind the pack), Elemental Orbs: Arc, and Tazer Tag form a self-reinforcing Arc-jolt cluster, while Counter Energy refunds the least-charged ability (your Grenade) on champ stuns to re-seed Bleak Watcher and the Arc Soul, and Press the Advantage / Reload at Range / Kinetic Synthesis keep your weapons online while three buddies passively clear. Tablet of Ruin (the other Arc-leaning artifact) is real competition but skews toward Bolt-Charge/heavy-weapon and Void/Strand DoT payoffs (Flashover, Defibrillating Blast, Void Flux) and lacks EDD's orb-generation + ability-regen + blind-CC density that a Grenade-stacked sustain GM build actually lives on. EDD wins on interaction count, not color.

## Gameplay Loop
1) Open by casting Healing Rift — this summons your Hellion (via the aspect) and starts class-ability regen. 2) Hold your grenade input to consume your Storm Grenade — Getaway Artist spawns your enhanced Arc Soul (making you Amplified) and Bleak Watcher turns the same consume into a Stasis turret that freezes/slows the room. You now have THREE buddies (Arc Soul, Hellion, Bleak Watcher) all dealing damage scaled by your 200 Grenade stat. 3) While the buddies are up, tap (don't hold) to throw spare Storm Grenades into the next pack — Facet of Dominance Jolts them for chain damage. 4) Play normally with your primary, popping Orbs to stay Amplified (Elemental Orbs: Arc) for survivability and ability regen. Facet of Courage adds +10% Light-ability/buddy damage against the frozen/slowed targets your turret creates. 5) Facets of Balance/Awakening + grenade mods (Bomber/Innervation/Firepower) refund grenade and class energy on kills and Orb pickups, so the turret and Arc Soul are nearly permanent. 6) Collect Light + Dark elemental pickups to fill Transcendence; use the Transcendent grenade for burst (it can't be consumed by Getaway Artist/Bleak Watcher, so re-seed your buddies with a regular held Storm Grenade once Transcendence ends). 7) On bosses/champions, drop the turret to freeze, let all three buddies focus-fire, and layer your Needlestorm super + heavy weapon for the damage phase. Champions are stunned simply by bringing the correct weapon frame (intrinsic anti-champ in 9.7.0).

## Strengths
Near-constant 3-4 active companions doing damage passively while you stay safe; exceptional ad-clear and boss chip damage; permanent crowd control via freeze/slow from Bleak Watcher; strong survivability stack (Healing Rift + near-permanent Amplified via Elemental Orbs: Arc + heal-on-Orb-pickup from Recuperation/Better Already + Dielectric's rapid-kill heals); huge ability uptime from grenade/Orb economy; scales extremely well into GMs because the buddies do work even when you're forced into cover. Benefits directly from the 9.7.0 change making buddies scale off the Grenade stat.

## Weaknesses
Your personal DPS is lower than dedicated damage builds — boss melt phases lean on weapons + super, not your kit. Buddy AI can mis-target or waste shots on trash. Requires very high Grenade investment (~200) to hit the buddy damage breakpoint, which crowds out other stats. Bleak Watcher turret has placement/uptime gaps if grenade energy stalls. Less single-target burst than the new double-Nova-Bomb Voidwalker or a dedicated Solar ignition build, so for pure boss-DPS speedruns it is a support/sustain pick rather than the top damage dealer.
