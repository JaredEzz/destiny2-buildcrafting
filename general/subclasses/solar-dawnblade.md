## Exotic — Dawn Chorus (Helmet)
Reworked in Update 9.7.0: scorch you apply builds toward STRONGER ignitions (bigger ignition damage), ignition damage now grants melee energy (with the internal cooldown on scorch energy gains removed), and Daybreak projectiles immediately ignite any target they hit. Combined with Kindling Trigger's x30/x45 scorch and Touch of Flame Fusion grenades, this turns every scorch source into a chained, self-sustaining ignition engine that also refunds your melee.

## Abilities
- **Super:** Daybreak (with Dawn Chorus, Daybreak projectiles instantly ignite targets on hit — best add-clear/boss-stagger super for this build; Well of Radiance remains the alternate team-DPS super if a support slot is needed)
- **Class Ability:** Healing Rift (Phoenix Dive is the alternate when you want faster Restoration application and Heat Rises uptime; Healing Rift is the GM/raid default for survivability)
- **Grenade:** Fusion Grenade
- **Melee:** Incinerator Snap (applies scorch in a cone, refunds melee energy on ignition damage via Dawn Chorus)

### Aspects
- Touch of Flame
- Heat Rises

### Fragments
- Ember of Ashes (more scorch stacks applied — boosts Kindling Trigger to x45 scorch, faster ignitions)
- Ember of Searing (defeating scorched targets grants melee energy and creates a Firesprite; fuels Incinerator Snap loop)
- Ember of Torches (powered melee hits make you and nearby allies Radiant — backup Radiant source and team buff)
- Ember of Empyrean (Solar weapon/ability kills extend Restoration and Radiant timers — near-infinite buff uptime)

*(Touch of Flame and Heat Rises grant two fragment slots each — four total.)*

## Weapons
- **Solar Wave-Frame GL (Demolitionist + Incandescent)** — your area-denial GL: Incandescent spreads Scorch (feeding ignitions/Firesprites) while Demolitionist refunds grenade energy. Excellent legendary fit for this build's Solar scorch/grenade economy.
- Vex Mythoclast (Solar precision kills feed scorch/ignitions; Kindling Trigger applies x30-45 scorch on every shot while Radiant)
- Polaris Lance / Sunshot (exotic primary alternatives that natively apply scorch + ignite for the same loop)
- Aggressive-frame Solar or Heavy-Burst Solar legendary (intrinsic Unstoppable under Anti-Champion 2.0 — no artifact mod needed)
- Precision/Adaptive-frame legendary primary (intrinsic Barrier-stun under the new frame-based champion system)
- Rocket-Assisted Frame Solar (e.g. for boss DPS — intrinsic Unstoppable, benefits from Revitalizing Blast Weaken)

## Stat Priority
Super > Grenade (~70) > Class > Health > Melee (~70) > Weapons. Push Super high for Daybreak/ignition uptime; hit ~70 Grenade and ~70 Melee so Fusion grenade + Incinerator Snap behave normally as ignition seeds; invest Class past 100 if possible for the on-cast overshield and stronger ability-energy economy. Health 100+ for the GM survivability layer (shield capacity + faster recharge). Weapons last.

## Artifact — Queensfoil Censer *(re-evaluated)*

Queensfoil Censer is genuinely best-in-slot here, and not because of "Solar element matching" — it is the only artifact in the pool that contains the full Radiant-to-Scorch-to-Ignition chain this exotic is built around. Dawn Chorus's 9.7.0 rework turns scorch into stronger ignitions, refunds melee on ignition damage, and instantly ignites Daybreak projectiles. The Censer supplies every input that engine consumes: Fever and Chill (Radiant on Solar precision), Kindling Trigger (Radiant makes Solar weapons apply scorch), and Rays of Precision (Radiant precision finals ignite directly). No other artifact (NPA = Void, Hunter's Journal/Slayer = Stasis-leaning, Tablet = Arc/Void/Strand, Encrypted Data Disk = Kinetic/Sword) carries Kindling Trigger + Rays of Precision + Torch, which are the load-bearing multipliers. Torch additionally turns the Radiant uptime into a flat weapon-damage bonus against Scorch-adjacent debuffs, and Heart of the Flame buffs Daybreak for the boss-DPS half of the build's job. It is the correct pick by mechanic interaction, period.

## Gameplay Loop
1) Open with a Solar primary (Vex/Sunshot/Polaris) precision hit to proc Flint Striker → you are Radiant. 2) Radiant + Kindling Trigger means every Solar shot now stacks x30-45 scorch on the whole pack. 3) Throw a Touch of Flame Fusion grenade and/or Incinerator Snap into the scorched group — Dawn Chorus makes the resulting ignitions hit much harder. 4) Ignition damage refunds melee energy (Dawn Chorus) and scorched-target kills refund melee + spawn Firesprites (Ember of Searing), so Incinerator Snap is almost always back. 5) Ember of Empyrean constantly extends Restoration and Radiant off every Solar kill, so you never drop your buffs. 6) Heat Rises (consume grenade) gives a burst of Cure, lets you stay airborne with improved in-air accuracy, and refunds melee energy on airborne kills. 7) For boss DPS: proc Revitalizing Blast to Weaken the boss, cast Daybreak — Dawn Chorus makes every projectile instantly ignite for huge sustained damage, while Heart of the Flame keeps your ability energy and Restoration topped up. 8) Rinse: precision hit keeps Flint Striker alive → infinite Radiant → infinite ignitions.

## Strengths
Exceptional, self-sustaining add-clear via chained ignitions that scale up hard with Dawn Chorus's rework. Near-permanent Radiant (Flint Striker, no cooldown) gives a constant +10-25% weapon damage and feeds the scorch engine. Constant Restoration + Healing Rift makes it very survivable in Master/GM content. Anti-Champion 2.0 means champions are handled intrinsically by weapon frame, freeing the whole artifact for Solar damage perks. Daybreak with Dawn Chorus and Revitalizing Blast Weaken gives credible boss DPS, not just add-clear. Great solo and team value (Ember of Torches Radiant sharing on powered melee hits).

## Weaknesses
Ignition output is gated behind maintaining Radiant — if you stop landing precision hits (e.g. against a target-poor encounter or with bad aim) the engine sputters. Lower single-target burst than dedicated DPS supers/setups; it is more of a sustained-damage and add-control build than a top-tier raid damage phase. Relies on a Solar precision primary, so it is less flexible with weapon choice than Prismatic. Ability-economy nerfs in 9.7.0 (active cooldown bonus cap cut to 125%) mean you must actually hit the ~70 Grenade/Melee stat targets or grenades feel slow. Daybreak is still a relatively weak roaming super for pure boss damage compared to Well/Song of Flame in coordinated groups.
