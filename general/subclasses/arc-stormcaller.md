## Exotic — Geomag Stabilizers
Damaging enemies with Chaos Reach extends its duration (up to ~120% more total damage per cast). Collecting Ionic Traces grants +7% bonus Super energy. Sprinting while holding 6+ Bolt Charge stacks grants additional Bolt Charge. Pairs with Ashes to Assets / Ionic Trace economy for very fast Super regen — turns Chaos Reach into a repeatable, sustained boss-DPS Super.

## Stat Priority
Super > Class > Weapons > Grenade > Health > Melee. Push Super past 100 for the extra Super-chunk layer (this build IS a Super-spam build); Class >100 for overshield-on-rift survivability; ~70 Grenade so Storm Grenade/Spark of Discharge behave normally. Health 100 for Orb HP. Melee is the dump stat.

## Artifact — Tablet of Ruin *(re-evaluated)*

Tablet of Ruin is the only artifact in the pool whose perk web is built around this loop's three pillars: Bolt Charge, Amplified, and Arc Super energy. Dielectric -> Flashover is a self-contained Bolt Charge -> Amplified -> bolt-damage engine; Photonic Flare/Defibrillating Blast layer Blind and refill Bolt Charge; Elemental Supercharger and Limit Break directly feed and buff Chaos Reach. No other artifact touches Bolt Charge at all - Hunter's Journal and NPA have scattered Arc perks (Shock and Awe, Bad Amplitude, Galvanic Armor) but no Bolt Charge or Arc-Super-energy column, so they cannot drive a Geomag Super-spam loop. This is mechanical fit, not element matching: the build literally cannot exist without Dielectric/Flashover/Elemental Supercharger, which only Tablet provides.

## Gameplay Loop
1) Open with Arc weapon kills to charge and deploy Ionic Sentry (Arc turret). 2) The Sentry Blinds on deploy and your Jolting Feedback primary jolts; ability kills (Storm Grenade / Ball Lightning) on those Arc-debuffed enemies are turned into Ionic Traces + Bolt Charge stacks by Electrostatic Mind and Dielectric. 3) Collecting Ionic Traces makes you Amplified (Electrostatic Mind) and dumps bonus Super energy via Geomag (+7%); Spark of Ions and Spark of Discharge keep the Traces flowing, and Ashes to Assets adds Super energy on Storm Grenade kills. 4) At max Bolt Charge, ability damage releases a heavy Arc bolt; Flashover keeps you permanently Amplified and boosts that bolt's damage. 5) When Super is up, fire Chaos Reach into the boss/ad-dense area — Geomag extends the beam while you keep damaging, for up to ~120% increased Super damage per cast. 6) Ionic Trace + Bolt Charge economy refills the Super fast, so the loop repeats with near-constant Chaos Reach uptime. Healing Rift + Amplified DR keep you alive between cycles.

## Strengths
Exceptional, repeatable single-target boss DPS via Geomag Chaos Reach (one of the strongest Super-spam builds in 9.7.0 after the +15% Chaos Reach buff and doubled Chain Lightning). Strong sustained ad-clear from the Ionic Sentry turret + jolt chains. Excellent survivability stack: near-permanent Amplified (damage resist), Healing Rift, and Class >100 overshields. Bolt Charge gives constant passive Arc bolts. Champion coverage is automatic now that anti-champ is intrinsic to weapon frames — no artifact mods needed. Great for GMs, raids, dungeons, and Onslaught.

## Weaknesses
Damage is front-loaded into the Super window — between Chaos Reach casts, single-target damage is mediocre and leans on your Heavy weapon. Chaos Reach requires standing relatively still while channeling, exposing you in high-pressure GM/boss-mechanic moments. The loop is fragment- and economy-dependent: if Ionic Trace / Bolt Charge generation stalls (low ad density or you miss kills), Super regen and Amplified uptime sag. Note the known Ionic Sentry compatibility quirks (interactions with some fragments/exotics have been buggy historically) — verify Spark of Ions and Electrostatic Mind procs in-game. Ball Lightning melee is awkward to land reliably.
