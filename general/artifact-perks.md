# Artifact Perk Index — Monument of Triumph (7 Artifacts)

> **Authoritative rebuild.** Perks + **tiers** from the live-game datamine (video-confirmed: every disagreement was a video T3-misread, never the datamine). Descriptions from the in-game SandboxPerk definitions. Ratings = Warlock PvE endgame (S/A/B/C/D) carried from prior analysis where available.
>
> **Equip rule:** 7 perk slots — **2 accept only Tier 1**, **3 accept Tier 1-2**, **2 accept Tier 1-2-3**. So a legal set has **≥2 Tier-1** and **≤2 Tier-3** perks. **No anti-champion perks** (intrinsic to weapon frames in 9.7.0).
