## Subclass builds

### Dawn Chorus Ignition (Solar)
- **Try first:** Vanguard Ops; Onslaught (ignitions chain in wave density); Altars/public events.
- **Shines:** Vault of Glass (Vex packs detonate before they touch the team), Crota's End Abyss, The Arms Dealer GM (Solar burn doubles your ignition output — mind taken damage too).
- **Friends:** Your Ember of Torches Radiant feeds the **GG Hunter's** Celestial shot; ignitions cover the **invis Hunter** while he revives; flex friend brings a Well or bubble for DPS.
- **Raid role:** add-clear specialist + passive Radiant battery — not the Well bot.

### Geomag Chaos Reach (Arc)
- **Try first:** featured strike playlist (traces flow off trash); Contest of Elders.
- **Shines:** Atheon, Templar, Taniks, Atraks — any stand-and-shoot DPS check; Insight Terminus GM (Vex/Cabal density feeds traces).
- **Friends:** Sequence supers — **GG burst** opens the phase, your beam sustains it. Invis Hunter handles a mechanic while you channel. Flex friend: Well/tether.
- **Raid role:** primary super-DPS dealer post-9.7.0 buffs.

### Soul Siphon Voidwalker (Skull)
- **Try first:** Expert Lost Sectors (the loop carries solo); Onslaught.
- **Shines:** Vault of Glass everywhere; Birthplace of the Vile GM (Void burn); add-dense dungeon encounters (Crota Abyss-style). Your best **all-rounder GM trio** pick.
- **Friends:** Your Weaken is live exactly when **GG** fires — free +15%. Devour anchor means the **invis Hunter** can play scout. Flex: Tractor Titan stacks debuff hierarchy (theirs overrides — coordinate).
- **Raid role:** debuff anchor + add engine; double Nova on burst checks.

### Osmiomancy Shadebinder (Stasis)
- **Try first:** Master Lost Sectors; Defiant Battlegrounds.
- **Shines:** **The Glassway GM** (freeze the Wyverns — the strike's whole problem), Exodus Crash GM (no-cover arena, turrets hold lanes), Chamber of Suffering (Pit of Heresy).
- **Friends:** Frozen targets are crit-stationary for **GG**; turret coverage lets the **invis Hunter** do objectives; flex friend brings DPS — you are control.
- **Raid role:** niche — champion/add lockdown wings (Desert Perpetual), otherwise bench.

### Necrotic Broodweaver (Strand)
- **Try first:** Vanguard Ops; Onslaught (Nest turret on the ADU lane).
- **Shines:** Crota's End (thrall = endless poison chain), The Disgraced GM (Hive hordes), Scarlet Keep GM corridors.
- **Friends:** You delete trash so **GG** saves the super for majors; poison chains finish what the **invis Hunter's** smokes tag; flex: boss-DPS loadout.
- **Raid role:** add-clear; modest boss damage — pair with DPS teammates.

### Getaway Prismatic (twin turrets)
- **Try first:** literally anything — this is the do-everything chassis. Seasonal content, Coil.
- **Shines:** **HyperNet Current GM** (Arc Soul covers the platforming gauntlet), Root of Nightmares node-running, Prophecy (turrets handle Taken while you dunk).
- **Friends:** Your turrets + Devour free both Hunters to play their game; Bleak Watcher slow stuns Overload for the trio.
- **Raid role:** utility/runner — the build you bring when your job is mechanics.

## Exotic helper builds

### Speaker's Sight (healing turret)
- **Try first:** matchmade Onslaught (turret on the ADU); seasonal activity.
- **Shines:** GM trio sustain (Lightblade, Warden of Nothing), Master VoG locked-loadout attrition, **Queenswalk (Last Wish)** and any raid wipe-risk phase.
- **Friends:** **Best-in-class with your trio** — turret heals the GG Hunter through his exposed super cast, and the invis Hunter ducks out with Restoration already ticking. Flex: damage, you're the healer.
- **Raid role:** first-class dedicated-healer slot.

### Boots of the Assembler (Noble Seekers)
- **Try first:** featured dungeon (normal) with the trio; Onslaught.
- **Shines:** spread-out fights where a stationary Well fails — Vow Acquisition, Riven, Salvation's Edge mechanics phases. Seekers chase allies you can't see.
- **Friends:** Seekers find the **invis Hunter** (healing without breaking stealth — genuinely his best partner); GG gets Radiant from your loop. Flex: bring the Well, you're the roaming heal.
- **Raid role:** healer slot for mechanic-heavy raids (Salvation's Edge especially).

### Getaway Artist (helper) — see Getaway Prismatic above; this file's variant leans harder into Arc Soul uptime for solo/neutral play.

### Mataiodoxia (suspend engine)
- **Try first:** Battlegrounds; Expert Lost Sectors with champions.
- **Shines:** **Warden of Nothing GM** (all three champ types — needles pierce Barrier, suspend stuns Unstoppable), Tormentor encounters (Vow, RoN), Lake of Shadows GM.
- **Friends:** Suspend holds the room mid-air while **GG** picks off priorities; suspended enemies can't shoot the **invis Hunter** out of a revive. Flex: DPS.
- **Raid role:** niche CC — bring for Tormentor-heavy or champion-heavy content.

### Starfire Protocol (fusion DPS)
- **Try first:** strike-boss farming; Grasp of Avarice Avarokk.
- **Shines:** sustained-DPS bosses with adds nearby — Golgoroth, Caiatl, **Ghosts of the Deep's giant HP pools**, Rhulk (grenades work while moving).
- **Friends:** Your Empowering Rift buffs **GG's** shot if he stands in it pre-cast; invis Hunter tethers; flex: Well so you can grenade-spam from inside it.
- **Raid role:** sustained boss DPS — the damage you deal *between* supers.

### Nezarec's Sin (Devour/Weaken)
- **Try first:** Expert Lost Sectors; Altars of Summoning.
- **Shines:** **Birthplace of the Vile GM** (Void burn supercharges everything), Salvation's Edge mechanic duty (you cannot die while executing), solo dungeons (Duality, Shattered Throne).
- **Friends:** Permanent Weaken uptime = standing +15% for both Hunters' damage; you never need saving, freeing Omni smokes for others.
- **Raid role:** the self-sufficient mechanics player who also debuffs.

### Rime-Coat Stasis Turret — see Osmiomancy Shadebinder; this variant trades Coldsnap spam for bigger zone-denial. Same homes: Glassway, Exodus Crash, Chamber of Suffering, Vesper's Host solo (Frost Armor is the best solo mitigation in the collection).

### Prismatic Class Item (Apotheosis + Star-Eater)
- **Try first:** Pantheon 2.0 early bosses; featured raid farm.
- **Shines:** **Pantheon 2.0 score runs** and every burst-DPS check — Riven, Oryx, Desert Perpetual wings. Overcharged Nova → Apotheosis ability dump is your highest one-window number.
- **Friends:** Stack your Nova inside the GG window on a Weakened boss (flex friend Tractors first) — that's the trio's kill-shot combo.
- **Raid role:** burst-DPS specialist.

### Skull Double Nova — see Soul Siphon Voidwalker; the helper file is the boss-DPS-tuned variant (Empowering Rift). Prefer it for raid DPS slots; prefer the subclass version for GMs.

### Well Lunafaction (team anchor)
- **Try first:** featured raid with the 6-man — this build only makes sense with allies.
- **Shines:** every stack-and-shoot DPS phase: Templar, Golgoroth pool, Atraks, Taniks, Crota sword phases, **Pantheon 2.0 gauntlets**, Desert Perpetual checks. 🔴 Solo: dead weight.
- **Friends:** The entire point — your Well + Lunafaction reload multiplies the **GG Hunter** and everyone else; flex friend Tractors (one debuff) and you've built the damage ceiling.
- **Raid role:** THE anchor. Someone has to be the Well; with Lunafaction it's a promotion, not a tax.

### Threadweaver's Loom (threadling swarm)
- **Try first:** Onslaught (three swarm sources defend lanes passively); Vanguard Ops.
- **Shines:** Sundered Doctrine (low boss HP, knowledge-check — swarm covers you while puzzle-solving), Sunless Cell GM, Crota Abyss. Luzaku heals make it a budget support.
- **Friends:** Swarm + heal-MG covers both Hunters' aggressive plays; flex: real boss DPS (your weakness).
- **Raid role:** add-control + off-healer hybrid.

### Hellion Eunoia (Phoenix Dive)
- **Try first:** Expert Lost Sectors (artillery solos them); patrol-tier anything.
- **Shines:** long-sightline fights — **Scarlet Keep GM** (Consul's Pitch wants 17m+), Lightblade approaches, Spire of the Watcher. Zero rift management by design.
- **Friends:** Turret pressure strips shields so **GG** crits unshielded targets; dive-cure keeps you off the Omni Hunter's rescue list. Flex: Well for DPS phases.
- **Raid role:** add-clear with the best passive uptime; modest DPS.

### Necrotic + Osteo Striga (poison chain)
- **Try first:** Altars of Summoning / Hive-anything; Onslaught.
- **Shines:** **Crota's End Abyss/Bridge** (the densest thrall in the game = the best poison chain in the game), The Disgraced GM, Scarlet Keep GM, solo dungeons with swarm pressure (Pit of Heresy).
- **Friends:** Poison + suspend hold the room so **GG** never wastes shots on trash; chain kills cover the **invis Hunter's** rotations. Flex: single-target DPS — your one gap.
- **Raid role:** add-clear king; bring a real heavy for the boss.

## Quick trio templates (you + GG Hunter + flex)
- **GM default:** Soul Siphon Voidwalker (you) + Celestial GG (burst) + flex on Titan bubble/Tractor. Swap you → **Rime-Coat** for Glassway/Exodus Crash, → **Mataiodoxia** for Warden of Nothing, → **Speaker's Sight** when attrition (Lightblade) is the killer.
- **Raid DPS check:** Well Lunafaction (you) + GG + flex Tractor → swap you → Skull/Class-item Nova when someone else Wells.
- **Carry the randoms:** Getaway Prismatic or Necrotic Osteo — builds that don't need teammates to function.
