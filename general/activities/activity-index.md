# Activity → Build Index — Monument of Triumph (June 2026)

> Every raid, dungeon, and GM-pool strike, with the best picks from the 19-build collection. Catalog researched June 2026 (post-9.7.0: full raid/dungeon catalogs are available; two of each featured weekly at Tier 5). **Team note** = what the Golden Gun Hunter / invis Hunter / flex friend bring.

## Raids (10)

| Raid | Best builds | Why / team note |
|---|---|---|
| **Vault of Glass** | Soul Siphon Voidwalker · Dawn Chorus · Well Lunafaction | Highest Vex add density in the catalog feeds Devour/ignitions; Templar+Atheon are stand-and-shoot → Well anchor. *Team: GG on Wyverns/minotaurs, flex Tractor for Atheon.* |
| **Crota's End** | Necrotic+Osteo · Well Lunafaction · Skull Double Nova | Abyss/Bridge thrall = the best poison chain in the game; Well centers sword-relay DPS; Skull bursts Ir Yut. *Team: invis Hunter is the natural sword carrier escort.* |
| **King's Fall** | Well Lunafaction (Golgoroth pool) · Skull/Class-item Nova (Oryx) · Threadweaver (Warpriest adds) | Golgoroth is a stand-in-pool check; Oryx wants tight burst windows. *Team: GG times with brands; flex runs plates.* |
| **Last Wish** | Nezarec's Sin (mechanics) · Class-item Nova (Riven burst) · Speaker's Sight (Queenswalk) | Long, mechanics-driven; survivable self-sufficient builds win. Speaker's Sight trivializes Queenswalk healing. |
| **Garden of Salvation** | Rime-Coat/Osmiomancy · Nezarec's Sin · Well Lunafaction | Native Overload Minotaurs even on Normal → Stasis slow stuns them; tether duty wants Devour; Sanctified Mind DPS is stand-and-shoot. |
| **Deep Stone Crypt** | Geomag Chaos Reach · Well Lunafaction · Getaway Prismatic (augment runner) | Beginner-friendly; Atraks/Taniks are generous DPS checks where Chaos Reach and Well shine. |
| **Vow of the Disciple** | Starfire (mobile Rhulk DPS) · Mataiodoxia (Tormentors) · Boots of the Assembler | Rhulk forces movement during DPS → grenades > stationary supers; suspend shuts down Tormentors; Seekers heal the spread-out Acquisition teams. |
| **Root of Nightmares** | Getaway Prismatic (node runner) · Well Lunafaction (Nezarec) · Mataiodoxia (Tormentors) | Lightest mechanics; Arc Soul covers the runners; Nezarec DPS is largely stand-and-shoot. |
| **Salvation's Edge** | Nezarec's Sin · Boots of the Assembler · Well Lunafaction | Mechanic execution during DPS → you must not die (Devour) and heals must chase people (Seekers). Not pug-friendly — bring the 6-man. |
| **The Desert Perpetual** | Well Lunafaction + Class-item Nova · Rime-Coat (wing control) | Newest raid, punishing DPS checks → bring the damage ceiling (Well + Tractor + burst Nova). Vex time mechanics favor zone control in wings. |

## Dungeons (11)

| Dungeon | Best builds (solo pick **bolded**) | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Shattered Throne** | **Necrotic+Osteo** · Starfire (Dul Incaru burn) | Lightest mechanics, classic first solo-flawless; poison chains the thrall waves, Starfire burns the boss. |
| **Pit of Heresy** | **Rime-Coat Stasis Turret** · Necrotic+Osteo | Chamber of Suffering is THE solo choke — turret zone-hold wins it; poison handles the rest of the Hive. |
| **Prophecy** | **Getaway Prismatic** · Dawn Chorus | Taken hit hard → turrets fight while you dunk motes; Kell Echo corridor favors sustained mobile damage. |
| **Grasp of Avarice** | **Nezarec's Sin** · Well (team Avarokk) | Devour through engram-debuff pressure; scorch-cannon DPS is forgiving with a Well. |
| **Duality** | **Soul Siphon Voidwalker** · Well Lunafaction (Caiatl) | Bell timers + Cabal density want Devour sustain; Caiatl is a big-crit stand-and-shoot check. |
| **Spire of the Watcher** | **Hellion Eunoia** · Starfire (Persys) | Easiest modern solo; long sightlines are exactly Consul's Pitch range. |
| **Ghosts of the Deep** | **Soul Siphon Voidwalker** · Starfire | Marathon boss HP → sustained grenade DPS + unkillable Devour loop; Lucent Lightbearers feed the kill economy. |
| **Warlord's Ruin** | **Rime-Coat** (blight storms) · Threadweaver | Zone control during storm holds; forgiving phases make it the trio's fun-run. |
| **Vesper's Host** | **Rime-Coat Stasis Turret** · Skull (Raneiks AoE) | The most punishing recent solo → Frost Armor is the collection's best solo mitigation; Cataclysm seekers shred the split servitor. |
| **Sundered Doctrine** | **Threadweaver's Loom** · Nezarec's Sin | Knowledge-check dungeon with low boss HP — the swarm covers you while you work the Lexicon. |
| **Equilibrium** | **Getaway Prismatic** · Well (team) | Newest dungeon (Dec 2025), Cabal + Dredgen mix — flexible multi-element chassis until the meta settles. |

## Grandmaster strikes (14 in pool)

| GM Strike | Champions | Best builds | Why / team note |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Exodus Crash** | Barrier, Overload | Rime-Coat / Osmiomancy | Zero-cover finale → freeze lanes; Slow stuns Overload. *GG deletes Barrier Hobgoblins; flex on bubble.* |

| **Warden of Nothing** | **All three** | Mataiodoxia | Needles pierce Barrier, suspend stuns Unstop, Shackle slows the rest — one build answers the triple-champ strike. |
| **Birthplace of the Vile** | Overload ×12, Unstop ×8 | Nezarec's Sin · Soul Siphon Voidwalker | Void burn supercharges the Devour engine; Suppress (Child alt) stuns Overload, ignite/suspend friends cover Unstop. |
| **Defiant Battleground: EDZ** | Overload, Unstop | Rime-Coat · Getaway Prismatic | Open battleground → turret zone control; Bleak Watcher slow handles Overload. |
| **The Disgraced** | Barrier, Unstop | Necrotic+Osteo · Dawn Chorus | Hive horde strike — poison/ignition chains; fire-pit modifier punishes melee range, so seed poison with Osteo not melee. |
| **The Sunless Cell** | Overload, Unstop | Threadweaver · Mataiodoxia | Blight geysers punish camping — swarm fights while you reposition; suspend covers both champ duties with frames. |
| **The Lightblade** | Barrier, Unstop | Speaker's Sight · Hellion Eunoia | Attrition strike (moths, screebs) → healing turret saves runs; Hellion shoots moths before they reach you. |
| **HyperNet Current** | Overload, Unstop | Getaway Prismatic | The platforming gauntlet is the danger — Arc Soul shoots while you jump; Bleak Watcher slow = Overload stun. |
| **The Insight Terminus** | Barrier, Unstop | Geomag Chaos Reach · Starfire | Vex/Cabal density feeds Ionic Traces; Kargen arena is a clean Chaos Reach lane. |
| **The Glassway** | Barrier ×12, Overload ×18 | **Rime-Coat / Osmiomancy** (signature) | The turret strike: freeze the Wyverns and the overwhelming Overload count. *Flex brings Barrier-frame weapons.* |
| **The Scarlet Keep** | Barrier, Unstop | Hellion Eunoia · Necrotic+Osteo | Long sightlines up the keep = Consul's Pitch territory; tight shaft sections = poison chain territory. |
| **Fallen S.A.B.E.R.** | Overload, Barrier | Rime-Coat · Speaker's Sight | Increased incoming damage modifier → control + sustain beats damage-race; Slow stuns the Overload Captains. |
| **Lake of Shadows** | Unstop, Overload | Getaway Prismatic · Mataiodoxia | Four shield elements → Prismatic flexibility; Taken Overload Captains hate Bleak Watcher slow + suspend. |
