# Artifact Perk Index — Monument of Triumph (7 Artifacts)

> MoT replaced the single seasonal artifact with **7 selectable artifacts**; you equip **one at a time** and pick its perks. Anti-Champion mods were **removed from artifacts** (stun is now weapon-frame intrinsic). Ratings are for **Warlock PvE endgame**: S = build-defining, A = strong, B = situational, C/D = skippable.

# Monument of Triumph — Warlock PvE Artifact "What's Good" Guide

## CRITICAL CONTEXT (changes the whole meta)
In Update 9.7.0 (June 9, 2026), **anti-champion was removed from artifacts entirely** and made **intrinsic to all weapons by frame archetype** (Aggressive/High-Impact/Heavy Burst/Wave/Micro-Missile/Rocket-Assisted = Unstoppable; Precision/Adaptive/Legacy/Caster/Support = Barrier; Lightweight/Rapid-Fire/Support-AR/Vortex/Overheat = Overload). You no longer pick an artifact for champion mods. **Also: Radiant and Volatile Rounds no longer STUN Barrier champions — they now grant +10% weapon damage vs. champions instead.** So the old "anti-barrier via Radiant/Volatile" stun loops are dead.

Each Monument artifact now holds **7 selectable high-impact sandbox perks** (column-gated; the old refund loop is gone, loadout-integrated, works in DIM; disabled in Comp/Trials). The 7 artifacts are returning seasonal artifacts, balance-passed and stripped of champion columns. So artifact choice is now purely about **which subclass/build buffs you stack.**

---

## BEST ARTIFACT PER SUBCLASS (Warlock PvE)

### SOLAR → **Queensfoil Censer** (S-tier, widely called the strongest artifact this year)
The premier Solar artifact. Stacks Radiant uptime, Scorch, and Ignitions.
- **Flint Striker** (must-have): Solar precision hits/final blows grant Radiant for 10s, no cooldown — near-permanent Radiant.
- **Kindling Trigger**: While Radiant, Solar weapons apply x30 Scorch — feeds ignitions.
- **Rays of Precision**: While Radiant, Solar precision final blows trigger Ignitions.
- **Heart of the Flame**: Solar Super grants nearby allies Radiant + up to +20% Super damage (great for Well/Song of Flame DPS).
- **Revitalizing Blast**: Solar ability damage Weakens champions/bosses (+15% all damage) — your in-build damage buff for boss DPS.
- Runner-up Solar option: **Hunter's Journal** (Radiant Orbs makes you Radiant on Orb pickup) if you prefer an Orb-economy build.

### ARC → **Tablet of Ruin** (S-tier for Arc)
Built around Bolt Charge / Amplified.
- **Dielectric**: Defeating Arc-debuffed (jolt/blind) targets grants Bolt Charge, spawns Orbs + heals.
- **Flashover** (standout): Become Amplified at max Bolt Charge and bolts deal more damage — keeps you Amplified passively.
- Alt: **Encrypted Data Disk** also has a Dielectric variant (Arc-debuff kills → Bolt Charge + Orb + heal) and **Elemental Orbs** (Arc kills spawn throwable Arc orbs) if you want a weapon-Arc build.

### VOID → **Tablet of Ruin** or **Encrypted Data Disk** (both strong)
- **Tablet of Ruin**: **Volatile Marksman** (Void precision/kills → Volatile Rounds, boosted grants class-ability energy on detonation) + **Void Flux** (defeating weakened targets makes nearby enemies Volatile) — exponential Volatile chaining.
- **Encrypted Data Disk**: **Power from Pain** (rapid kills on weakened enemies grant Devour), **Singularity Blade** (Void buffs let melee weaken; final blows = weakening burst), **Void Infestation** (defeating a weakened target spawns a tracking weakening projectile). This is the artifact for the **Soul Siphon Void Warlock** melee/Devour loop that's the hottest Warlock build of the season.

### STASIS → **Queensfoil Censer** (best Stasis support) or **Implement of Curiosity**
- **Queensfoil Censer**: **Pillar of Ice** (defeating encased targets spawns Stasis crystals; bosses spawn two) + **Hail the Storm** (shattering crystals/encased targets deals bonus damage + Slowing shards) — superb for Bleak Watcher/shatter builds.
- **Implement of Curiosity**: **One with Frost**, **Frost Renewal**, **Frigid Glare**, **Fever and Chill** for a Frost Armor / freeze-centric build.

### STRAND → **Tablet of Ruin** (best) or **Queensfoil Censer**
- **Tablet of Ruin**: **Unraveling Orbs** (Tangle/Orb pickup → Unraveling Rounds) + **Horde Shuttle** (damaging unraveled targets spawns Threadlings) — drives Threadling/Unravel builds even with weapon-Strand.
- Queensfoil Censer also lists Strand among its favored subclasses if you're pairing Strand with Solar weapons.

### PRISMATIC → **Hunter's Journal** (the Prismatic/Transcendence artifact)
- **Transference** (must-have): Melee/grenade deal more damage while Transcendent + regain Light/Dark energy on kills = best Transcendence uptime.
- **Radiant Orbs**: Orb pickup → Radiant (huge on Prismatic Solar variants).
- **Galvanic Armor**: Damage reduction while Amplified (top-tier survivability on Prismatic Arc builds).
- Tablet of Ruin is also a strong Prismatic alternative (it lists Prismatic among favored subclasses) for Volatile/Bolt-Charge Prismatic kits.

## UNIVERSAL BEST PERKS (good across most Warlock builds)
1. **Flint Striker** (Queensfoil) — free, no-cooldown permanent Radiant on any Solar-weapon build.
2. **Transference** (Hunter's Journal) — best-in-slot for any Prismatic/Transcendence build.
3. **Galvanic Armor** (Hunter's Journal) — flat damage reduction while Amplified; DR is always king in endgame.
4. **Heavy Ordnance / Heavy Ordinance Regeneration** (Tablet of Ruin) — sustained weapon damage grants DR + grenade & melee regen; the go-to for ability-spam Warlocks.
5. **Solo Operative** (Queensfoil) — +15% to weapons AND abilities when soloing — best solo/dungeon perk in the game.
6. **Counter Energy** (Encrypted Data Disk) — champion stuns refund energy to your least-charged ability (now that everyone stuns champions with weapons, this triggers constantly).
7. **Revitalizing Blast** (Queensfoil) — Solar ability damage Weakens champions/bosses (+15% damage), a self-contained DPS buff.

---

## BEST CHAMPION-RELATED PERKS (post-overhaul)
Note: there are **no anti-champion stun perks on artifacts anymore** — stuns come from your weapon frame. The remaining champion-interaction perks instead **reward** stunning:
- **Counter Energy** (Encrypted Data Disk) — best: ability energy on every champion stun.
- **Revitalizing Blast** (Queensfoil) — Weakens champions with Solar ability damage (+15% damage taken).
- General tip: pair a Precision/Adaptive primary (Barrier) with a Lightweight/Rapid-Fire special or Aggressive heavy (Overload/Unstoppable) to cover all three champion types without spending any artifact slots.

---

## SLEEPER / UNDERRATED PERKS
- **Power from Pain** (Encrypted Data Disk) — free Devour on any subclass via rapid kills on weakened enemies; massive survivability for non-Void Warlocks who can apply weaken/debuffs.
- **Void Infestation** (Encrypted Data Disk) — turns single weakened-target kills into chaining weaken projectiles; quietly excellent ad-clear + debuff spread.
- **Counter Energy** — under-discussed but now that ALL weapons stun champions, this is one of the strongest ability-regen perks in endgame.
- **Heart of the Flame** (Queensfoil) — team Radiant + up to +20% Super damage; sleeper pick for Song of Flame / Well DPS rotations.
- **Solo Operative** — overlooked by team players but the single best perk for solo dungeon/legend content.
- **Singularity Blade** (Encrypted Data Disk) — melee-weaken + weakening burst pairs beautifully with the Soul Siphon Void melee loop for a self-sustaining weaken engine.
