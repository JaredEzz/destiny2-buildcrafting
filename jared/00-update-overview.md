# DESTINY 2: MONUMENT OF TRIUMPH — WARLOCK PvE BRIEF (Update 9.7.0, launched June 9 2026)

Bungie's final major content update. Sources: Titanquisitor dev-insight + patch-note articles, Kyber's Corner, Epiccarry, Boostmatch, Mobalytics, Blueberries.gg, Skycoach. June 2026 dating confirmed.

## 1) NEW / CHANGED WARLOCK ABILITIES, ASPECTS, SUPERS, EXOTICS

**New Aspect — Soul Siphon (Voidwalker / Void):**
- Land a powered melee to "ready" the aspect; press melee again to siphon energy from up to ~4 enemies in front of you.
- Each target hit deals damage and grants Void Overshield + class-ability energy.
- Damage scales with the enhanced benefit of the **Melee stat** (incentivizes Melee >100).

**Reworked Supers / Subclass abilities:**
- **Voidwalker — Skull of Dire Ahamkara exotic + Nova Bomb:** After your initial Nova Bomb cast you get a brief window to cast a second Nova Bomb (Nova Bomb: Lance / "Nova Lance"). Destroying a Cataclysm Nova Bomb with the follow-up Nova Lance spawns a Nova Vortex alongside the tracking seekers. This is the headline "double Nova Bomb" combo.
- **Voidwalker — Child of the Old Gods (aspect):** now Suppresses combatants and moves 100% faster.
- **Broodweaver (Strand) — Mindspun Invocation:** holding the grenade input now spawns a Threadling Nest.
- **Dawnblade (Solar) — Heat Rises:** now reduces enemy accuracy in PvE.
- **Stormcaller (Arc) — Chain Lightning:** damage doubled, and now grants 2 charges by default.

**Exotic armor reworks (Warlock):**
- **Skull of Dire Ahamkara** — enables the double Nova Bomb / Nova Lance combo above (top-tier change).
- **Praxic Vestments** — ~500% melee damage increase.
- Tuning passes also hit **Nezarec's Sin, Crown of Tempests, Dawn Chorus, Mantle of Battle Harmony, Astrocyte Verse, Contraverse Hold, Nothing Manacles.**
- **All Armor 3.0 exotics now drop at Tier 5 stats** and unlock all tuning mods — no re-grind for better rolls.

## 2) ARMOR 3.0 / STAT SYSTEM (how stats now work for Warlocks)

- **Six stats: Health, Melee, Grenade, Super, Class, Weapons.** Tiers are gone; every point counts. Stats scale **0–200**, with a second effect layer unlocked past 100.
- **Class stat** (formerly Recovery, the Warlock-critical one): 1–100 = faster class ability + better energy gain from all sources; **101–200 = overshield on every class-ability cast.** Several new abilities (incl. Soul Siphon synergy) scale with Class >100.
- **Health:** 1–100 = up to 70 HP per Orb + 10% flinch resist; 101–200 = +20 shield capacity (PvE), 25% faster shield start, 50% faster full recharge.
- **Grenade / Melee / Super:** govern base ability cooldown plus the "chunk" scalars from mods/perks. General build target: **70 Grenade and 70 Melee** to make weapon perks/subclass verbs behave normally; beyond 70 only if grenade/melee is your damage focus.
- **Weapons:** controls how fast Special/Heavy ammo HUD bars fill.
- **New armor archetypes:** six added (now 12 total) — Siegebreaker (Health/Grenade), Skirmisher (Melee/Weapons), Demolitionist (Grenade/Class), Colossus (Super/Health), Reaver (Class/Melee), Powerhouse (Weapons/Super). Ghost Armorer mod raises target-archetype odds to 1-in-2 (was 1-in-3).
- **Set bonuses:** trigger at 2 and 4 pieces; can run two 2-piece bonuses or 4-piece + exotic.
- **Ability-economy nerfs (all classes):** passive cooldown bonus cap cut ~20%; **active cooldown bonus cap dropped 190% → 125%.** Perfect T5 armor yields ~515 base stat points pre-exotic/MW/mods; masterworking adds ~75 to off-archetype stats.

## 3) ANTI-CHAMPION OVERHAUL (Anti-Champion 2.0)

- Champion counters are now **intrinsic to weapon frames — permanent, no seasonal Artifact mods required.** Just hit the target; effect applies immediately, no charge/activation condition.
- **Unstoppable:** Aggressive, High-Impact, Heavy Burst, Wave, Micro-Missile, Rocket-Assisted frames (e.g., Xenophage, Jötunn).
- **Barrier:** Precision, Adaptive, Legacy, Caster, Support frames (e.g., Izanagi's Burden, Parasite).
- **Overload:** Lightweight, Rapid-Fire, Support AR, Vortex, Overheat frames (e.g., Trinity Ghoul, Buried Bloodline).
- **Stasis change:** shatter now stuns **Unstoppable** (reverted from Overload) — relevant to Warlock Stasis (Bleak Watcher/Coldsnap shatter setups).

## 4) WEAPON TIER UPGRADING

- New mod-socket on the weapon inspect screen upgrades any weapon's gear tier (T1→T5) using **Glimmer, Ascendant Alloys, Enhancement Prisms.**
- **Tier 3:** unlocks Enhanced Mods (all weapons). **Tier 5:** enhanced perks/mods, enhanced barrel+mag, Masterwork stat boost, Combat Flair cosmetics.
- Limitation: upgrading **cannot add perks to existing columns**; multi-perk columns only on organic T5 drops.
- Crafted weapons tier up via the Crafting Table at weapon levels 11 / 25 / 30.

## 5) CURRENT WARLOCK PvE META (post-9.7.0)

**Strongest overall:**
- **Solar Warlock (Dawnblade) — Starfire Protocol:** still the safest top-tier all-rounder. Best-in-slot grenade uptime (Fusion Grenades), constant healing, Well of Radiance team buff + strong boss DPS. #1 recommendation for raids, GMs, and solo dungeons.
- **Prismatic Warlock — Getaway Artist:** premier do-everything build. Arc Soul + Feed the Void (perma-Devour) + Bleak Watcher turret for area denial, with Song of Flame super for team buffs/Scorch spam. Top general-content pick.

**Rising / strong post-update:**
- **Void Warlock (Voidwalker)** is meaningfully buffed by **Soul Siphon** (melee → overshield + class energy loop, Melee-stat scaled) and the **Skull of Dire Ahamkara double Nova Bomb** burst combo — strongest single-target Void Warlock nuke option this patch. Contraverse Hold remains the grenade-spam pick (DR while charging Chaos Accelerant, grenade energy + Weaken on hit).
- **Arc (Stormcaller)** improved via doubled, 2-charge Chain Lightning; Geomag Stabilizers strong vs bosses (Chaos Reach uptime + Ionic Trace super energy).

**Meta caveat:** the 60% boss-damage super-energy nerf and the 190%→125% active-cooldown cut rein in super/ability spam, nudging Warlocks toward sustained grenade/melee loops (Solar Starfire, Void Soul Siphon/Contraverse, Prismatic Getaway Artist) over super-chaining.

Key exotics to prioritize for Warlock PvE: **Starfire Protocol, Getaway Artist, Skull of Dire Ahamkara (newly buffed), Contraverse Hold, Geomag Stabilizers, Karnstein Armlets** (melee survivability).
