# Teammate Builds — What Your Friends Should Run Alongside Yours

> Companion picks for team content, built around the **stacking buckets**: one **empower** (highest wins: Banner 40% > Well 25% > Radiant 20%), one **debuff on the target** (Tractor/Tether 30% > Weaken 15%), **surges + Particle Reconstruction stack separately**, and **orbs are a shared economy** — every orb a teammate makes feeds your Super stat multiplier. Champion duty splits across the *fireteam's* weapon frames now, so coordinate frames, not mods.

---

## 🎯 For the Golden Gun / invis Hunter (friend A)

### A1. Celestial Nighthawk — the one-shot artillery *(his Golden Gun, weaponized by you)*
**Solar Hunter · Celestial Nighthawk · Golden Gun (Marksman)** — one shot, massive burst, refunds super energy on kill.
**Loop:** stay Radiant via knife (Knock 'Em Down), bank the super for the moment that matters; Acrobat's Dodge alt for team Radiant.
**Pairs with yours:** ⭐ **Triple-Det / Soul Siphon** — your Echo of Undermining Weaken (+15%) is permanently up, so his shot always lands amplified; your double Nova + his GG are alternating burst windows. ⭐ **Rain of Fire** — your Particle Reconstruction ×5 (~28%, separate bucket) makes his GG hit absurdly hard on fusion-softened bosses. ⭐ **Last Well** — Radiant + Lunafaction reload for his follow-up heavy.
**Content:** GM champion deletes, Desert Perpetual DPS checks, Pantheon boss rush.

### A2. Omnioculus smoke support — the rescue specialist *(his beloved invis, made team-first)*
**Void Hunter · Omnioculus · any super (Deadfall if he'll cast it)** — smoke makes the team invisible + DR; he gets melee refunds for being invisible.
**Loop:** smoke the team on wipes/revives, vanish, pick people up, repeat. The classic.
**Pairs with yours:** ⭐ **Rime-Coat / Plaguebearer GM anchors** — you hold the zone, he ghosts through it for objectives/revives; your poison/freeze covers his reposition. ⭐ **Speaker's Sight** — your Seekers heal him *without breaking invis*; the two of you make the trio nearly unwipeable. ⚠️ **Bucket note:** if he runs the Weaken-smoke fragment, his 15% and your Undermining 15% share the debuff slot — no loss, but no gain; one of you can drop it.
**Content:** GM trio (Lightblade, Warden of Nothing), Salvation's Edge mechanics, flawless attempts.

### A3. Orpheus Rig Deadfall — the team multiplier *(when he'll trade GG for value)*
**Void Hunter · Orpheus Rig · Shadowshot: Deadfall** — tether = **30% debuff** + suppress + a fountain of orbs refunding his super.
**Pairs with yours:** ⭐ **Apotheosis Eater** — his orb fountain overcharges your Star-Eater stacks *and* feeds your Super stat economy → more overcharged Novas. ⭐ **The Last Well (Loadout B)** — tether replaces Tractor as the team debuff, freeing your heavy for Apex/Gjallarhorn. His 30% overrides your 15% Weaken — that's an upgrade, not a conflict.
**Content:** raid DPS phases (Templar, Atraks, Taniks), Onslaught high waves, Crota's End.

### A4. Gyrfalcon's Hauberk — invis with teeth *(the aggressive invis alternative)*
**Void Hunter · Gyrfalcon's · Stylish Executioner** — leaving invis grants **Volatile Rounds** (+ reserve overshields for the team with Vanishing Step play).
**Pairs with yours:** ⭐ **Soul Siphon / Nezarec's** — double Volatile spread saturates rooms; his finisher overshields stack onto your Devour sustain for a near-immortal Void duo.
**Content:** GMs with heavy add pressure (Glassway, Exodus Crash), dungeons.

### A5. Wormhusk Crown — the budget medic *(NEW this patch: reworked)*
**Any-element Hunter · Wormhusk Crown** — 9.7.0 rework: **dodging "greatly cures" him AND nearby allies.**
**Pairs with yours:** any of your non-support builds — he becomes the pocket healer so you don't have to slot Speaker's; great with **Plaguebearer/Broodweaver** where you're face-tanking a chokepoint and his dodge tops you off.
**Content:** GM trio when you want triple-damage loadouts with no dedicated healer.

## 🔧 For the flex friend (friend B — Warlock/Titan/anything)

### B1. Cenotaph Mask Warlock — the ammo battery *(quietly the best raid support in the game)*
**Solar Warlock · Cenotaph Mask · trace rifle** — his trace marks a target; **teammate kills on it drop Heavy ammo for the whole team** + finisher special bricks.
**Pairs with yours:** ⭐ **Rain of Fire** (Cataclysmic never runs dry — your one scarce resource, solved), ⭐ **Last Well Loadout B** (Apex spam funded), ⭐ **Triple-Det** (Void heavy uptime). With him feeding heavy and you feeding orbs, the whole fireteam's economy closes.
**Content:** raids and long GM boss fights — anywhere heavy ammo is the bottleneck.

### B2. Ursa Furiosa Banner Shield Titan — the damage ceiling
**Void Titan · Ursa Furiosa · Sentinel Shield (Banner)** — **Weapons of Light: +40% team damage**, the strongest empower in the game; guarded damage refunds his super.
**Pairs with yours:** ⚠️ his 40% **overrides your Well's 25%** (same bucket) — so when he banners, you don't bring The Last Well; bring **Starfire / Rain of Fire / Stormwrath** and shoot through his shield instead. Tractor or tether still stacks on top (debuff bucket), and your Particle Recon stacks on *that*.
**Content:** the hardest DPS checks — Desert Perpetual wings, The Witness, Pantheon score runs.

### B3. Thundercrash Cuirass Titan — the other nuke
**Arc Titan · Cuirass of the Falling Star · Thundercrash** — body-slam burst on a short cooldown.
**Pairs with yours:** alternates burst windows with your **Triple-Det** (his slam → your double Nova → his slam again); your Weaken amps every impact. Simple, devastating cadence for dungeon bosses.
**Content:** dungeons (Vesper's Raneiks, Warlord's Hefnd), Pantheon.

### B4. Speaker's Sight Warlock — mirror medic *(when he's on Warlock and the content is brutal)*
He runs **your own Speaker's Sight build** (it's in the gist — one DIM click for him). Two healing turrets + your damage build of choice = the trio never dies.
**Pairs with yours:** frees YOU to play Triple-Det/Plaguebearer in content where you'd otherwise be stuck healing.
**Content:** Master raids, day-one-style content, GM weeks with brutal modifiers.

## ⚠️ Coordination rules (the 30-second team talk)
1. **One empower:** Banner (40%) > your Well (25%) > Radiant. If B banners, you swap off Well to a DPS build.
2. **One debuff:** tether/Tractor (30%) > anyone's Weaken (15%). Decide who carries it; your **Particle Reconstruction stacks on top regardless** (weapon-specific bucket).
3. **Champion frames:** split coverage across the *team's* weapon frames before the strike — your build files list each GM's champion mix.
4. **Orbs are communal:** your Siphon mods + his Orpheus refunds + Powerful Attraction = everyone's supers come back faster. Stand near each other.

> ⚠️ Hunter exotic specifics (Nighthawk/Orpheus/Gyrfalcon numbers) are from the established sandbox + MoT research where noted (Wormhusk rework is confirmed 9.7.0); have your Hunter verify current tooltips — Hunter builds weren't deep-researched like the Warlock 20.
