## 5. DPS Rotation — second-by-second

**Pre-phase (build the engine):**
- Glaive **melee final blows + finishers** on adds → **10 Winter's Guile stacks** (finishers +3 = fastest; ~3–4 finishers or ~10 kills). Stacks decay 1 / 4.75s.
- **Delay the stack-consuming melee until ~1–2s before the phase opens** so Warlord's Fury (10s) overlaps the super window perfectly.

**T = 0s:**
1. **Tractor Cannon** the boss → ~30% weaken.
2. **Cast Song of Flame.** Now stacked: Warlord's Fury (+400%, 38× regen) × Song of Flame buff × Tractor 30% × 200 Melee × Facet of Courage.

**T = 0–10s (the melt):**
3. **Spam Celestial Fire nonstop** — regen × Warlord's Fury = near-zero cooldown; bolts + scorch → big ignitions (Ruin) on a scorched+weakened boss.
4. **Hellion** auto-fires the whole time (free 2nd source).
5. **Phoenix Dive** the instant you take damage → Restoration + re-summon Hellion + arm Hope.
6. **Refresh Tractor** mid-window if vuln drops (single biggest avoidable DPS loss).
7. Kill nearby adds to slightly **extend** the window past 10s.

**T ≈ 10s:** Warlord's Fury ends (resets stacks to 0). **Swap to heavy + Tractor** for residual damage while you rebuild stacks + super.

**Get Song of Flame back fast:** Super 200 > orbs (Siphon + Powerful Attraction) > add kills (super is kill-driven + rebuilds WG stacks) > Transcendence as gravy.

## 6. Tips, Failure Modes & When to Use It

**It WINS when:**
- **Solo** content, where you don't have a fireteam's worth of rockets — a 10s, 2M+ window on a weakened boss is exceptional (soloed Zo'aurc in 2 phases).
- Stand-and-melee bosses with a steady add supply to rebuild stacks.

**It LOSES when:**
- **Group DPS** — a Bait & Switch rocket / LMG runs 20–35s/phase (~8–15M total) and usually beats the ~10s melee window on *total* damage. Peak per-second is higher; total-per-phase is not.
- **Melee-hostile bosses** (Atraks, Rhulk, Nezarec, Caretaker, Templar, most Contest): one-shot slams/knockbacks forbid standing in the box. **Some bosses are simply no-go — don't force it.**

**Failure modes (4 gates must align):**
1. **No adds in the DPS room** = can't build/refresh stacks = dead engine. Plan a DIM ad-clear loadout per encounter.
2. **Super not up** at phase start = you do nothing (creator admits non-super melee DPS is "quite low").
3. **Mistimed Warlord's Fury** — if the 10s doesn't overlap the super, you halve the build.
4. **Lapsed Tractor** — the 30% is multiplicative on Celestial Fire, Hellion, *and* ignitions.

**Reality check:** "Strongest sustained DPS in the game" is **marketing, not measured** — it's an elite **solo burst-melt window**, not a sustained king. Tractor sits in your Power slot purely for the weaken (no damage heavy), which is the tacit admission this is a melee-window build, not a sustained-DPS king.
