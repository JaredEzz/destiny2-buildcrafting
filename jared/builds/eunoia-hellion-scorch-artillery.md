# 🔥 Eunoia — Hellion Scorch-Artillery Warlock (DPS + add-clear)

**Fantasy:** Drop a Solar mortar, blanket the room in scorch, and watch it chain-ignite into wall-to-wall explosions.

**Verdict:** Legend-capable now; Master-capable *only after* you verify champion mods (see §8) and bump survivability. Genuinely strong, ramp-up add-clear — **not** a real boss-DPS build (your heavy does the boss work).

**Fun: 9/10 · Power: 6/10** (excellent add-clear, artifact-dependent, ~0 native boss DPS, unverified champ coverage).

**Subclass winner: SOLAR (Dawnblade).** Why: only Solar gives **Ember of Singeing** (scorch → faster class-ability recharge = more Hellion uptime) and the full Ember package that feeds the **Solar Fulmination** chain-ignition loop. Prismatic is the fun CC/turret-spam alt, not the main.

**Hellion availability (confirmed framing):** Hellion is a **native Dawnblade/Solar Aspect** (also in Prismatic's Solar slice) — it is **class-ability-summoned**, NOT Prismatic-only. ⚠️ Verify on your character screen in-game before committing.

## 3 · Weapons

**Equipped trio (all owned):**
- **Kinetic — Witherhoard:** fire-and-forget DoT; covers the build's sustained single-target blind spot at zero attention cost.
- **Energy (Solar) — Sunshot:** ignition-on-kill → feeds Solar Fulmination chains. The add-clear heart.
- **Heavy (Solar) — Dragon's Breath:** on-theme room-wide ignition seeder + a *passable* boss burn. (It's the *thematic* boss tool, not a great one — see §8.)

**Backups (owned):**
- **Prometheus Lens** (energy) — run with the **Last Discipline 4-pc** orb→special-ammo loop for a sustained beam-scorch playstyle.
- **Skyburner's Oath** (energy) — hipfire AoE scorch, no ammo worries.
- **Polaris Lance** (energy) — reliable precision-chain ignition on free ammo.
- **~19 Incandescent legendaries** — ammo-rich scorch spreaders if you'd rather not run an exotic primary.
- **Touch of Malice** (kinetic) — stronger primary boss DPS if you need it over Witherhoard.

**Chase (not owned):** **Fafnir** (exotic LFR, Monument of Triumph) — built-in **weaken**; the real boss answer. Swap in by encounter when you need a DPS phase.

## 6 · Armor Mods

- **Orb generation:** Reaper (1) / **Harmonic Siphon** matching your weapon elements — keeps the dive→Hellion loop spinning.
- **Last Discipline 4-pc (Power Loader)** — orb pickup = +6.25% special-ammo. Run this if using **Prometheus Lens** to never starve the trace.
- **Bomber** (class item) + **Insulation/Distribution** — orbs/pickups → faster grenade & class recharge (more Hellion).
- **Absolution** (if available) — ability energy on orb pickup, all-around loop fuel.
- **Survivability:** Resist mods (Concussive Dampener / elemental resist) — you live inside the explosions.
- **Font of Wisdom / Grenade-focused mods** to push the G stat's effective uptime.

## 7 · Gameplay Loop

**Add-clear a room:**
1. **Phoenix Dive** → summons **Hellion** mortar.
2. Hellion lobs → **Eunoia** splits each shot into scorching shrapnel; **Ember of Ashes** stacks scorch fast.
3. Targets hit ~100 scorch → **Ignition**. **Solar Fulmination** makes it bigger + lets it trigger *more* ignitions; **Ember of Char** re-scorches neighbors → **room-wide chain**.
4. **Sunshot** kills add ignition-on-kill detonations into the chain.
5. Every scorch tick feeds **Ember of Singeing** → class ability recharges → **dive again → new Hellion**. Pick up orbs to keep it spinning.

**DPS a boss (the honest version):**
1. The subclass contributes ~nothing to single-target — **your heavy does the work.**
2. **Dragon's Breath** now (seeds a burn) or **Fafnir** (chase; weakens the boss for the whole team).
3. Keep Hellion + a grenade on the boss for chip; pop **Song of Flame** for Radiant (flat weapon-damage buff into the phase).
4. **Witherhoard** ticks throughout for free sustained damage.

## 8 · Tips / Failure Modes

- ⚠️ **CHAMPIONS UNVERIFIED — #1 blocker.** Confirm Unstoppable / Overload / **Barrier** sources on Hunter's Journal *before* any champion content. Scorch likely covers Unstoppable; **Barrier is probably gun-dependent** — slot anti-barrier on a primary if the artifact doesn't supply it.
- **It ramps — it does NOT hit the ground running.** Cold-open against a fresh room is slow; the loop snowballs. In short/bursty waves it may still be warming up. **Your guns carry; Hellion amplifies** — don't expect an always-up turret.
- **Verify Grenade→Hellion scaling** before going 200 G. If the scaling is weak, make **Class** your #1 (uptime is guaranteed value).
- **Touch of Flame vs Heat Rises is a real choice, not "both."** Touch of Flame = more ignitions (lower content); Heat Rises = real heal-on-dive + air game (Master). You can't run both with Hellion.
- **Survivability is thin** — no Devour, only Phoenix Dive heal. Raise Health to ~100, keep some Super for Song of Flame as your reset. Beware the **Solar Fulmination self-damage bug** (reported lethal on relic/boss ignitions).
- **Stop treating Dragon's Breath as a real boss tool** — it's thematic, mid-tier DPS. Bring a meta heavy for actual phases.
- **Expiration date:** this build is Solar-Fulmination-dependent — a final-season artifact perk. It degrades hard once the artifact rotates.

**Fallback — Prismatic "Double-Turret Artillery":** Hellion + **Bleak Watcher** (two turrets at once). Fragments: **Facet of Ruin** (bigger ignitions/shatters), **Facet of Justice** (Transcendent ability-kills explode) + a survivability facet. More burst + freeze-CC, but you lose Singeing/Char so the chain is less self-sustaining. Run it when you want CC and nonstop turret coverage over the pure ignition loop.
