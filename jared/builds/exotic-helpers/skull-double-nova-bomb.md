## Why Voidwalker (not Prismatic)
The loop is entirely native-Void: it leans on **Devour** as a heal/super engine and the new Voidwalker-exclusive **Soul Siphon** aspect. Skull's reworked perk only functions on the Void Nova Bomb supers, so Prismatic offers no upside that beats Devour + Soul Siphon. (Prismatic "works" but is strictly worse for this loop.)

## Super — Nova Bomb: Cataclysm (keystone)
Skull's reworked perk is **Cataclysm-specific**: throw the slow seeking Cataclysm, then immediately fire the free **Nova Bomb: Lance** *into* it. The Lance shattering the Cataclysm = a **triple-detonation** (initial blast + seeker sub-munitions + a bonus Nova Vortex from the collision). Final-blow *count* drives the refund loop, so Cataclysm (many discrete kills) beats Vortex (single lingering well).

## Abilities
- **Class Ability:** Empowering Rift (committed — Devour + Soul Siphon overshields carry survival, so you stay in the Empowering damage zone without swapping rifts).
- **Grenade:** Vortex Grenade (Devour/volatile uptime; default) — Axion Bolt for dense add rooms.
- **Melee:** Pocket Singularity — required to ready **Soul Siphon**.

### Aspects
1. **Feed the Void** — powered ability final blows grant **Devour** (heal + grenade energy + the prerequisite for Skull's weapon-kill super refund).
2. **Soul Siphon** (new) — powered-melee hit readies it; press melee to siphon nearby foes for damage, **Overshield, and class-ability energy**. The between-supers filler.

### Fragments
- **Echo of Instability** — grenade kills → Volatile Rounds (feeds Devour + weapon super energy).
- **Echo of Undermining** — grenades weaken → amplifies all Nova Bomb damage.
- **Echo of Persistence** — extends your Void buffs (Devour/Overshield) so the loop doesn't lapse.
- **Echo of Harvest** — weakened-target kills spawn Void Breaches → class-ability energy (more Rift uptime).
- **Echo of Reprisal** — final blows while surrounded grant super energy (stacks on Skull).

> Non-negotiable three: **Instability + Undermining + Reprisal.** Drop Harvest/Persistence if a stat penalty hurts your Super target.

## Exotic — Skull of Dire Ahamkara (Actual Grandeur)
> *After casting a Nova Bomb, a short window lets you cast a free instant-charge **Nova Bomb: Lance**. Destroying a Cataclysm with a Lance creates a **Nova Vortex** alongside the seekers. Gain Super energy on Nova Bomb final blows, and on weapon final blows while you have Devour.*

Two refund clauses make it self-sustaining: (1) every Nova Bomb final blow returns super (triple-detonation makes many); (2) weapon final blows under Devour also return super. Next Nova Bomb in ~15s of active add-clear.

## Weapons
- **Bad Juju** (Exotic Pulse, Kinetic) — effectively mandatory. **String of Curses** grants super energy directly + refreshes Devour; the best weapon-side super battery, stacks with Skull's refund. **#1 pick.**
- **Void legendary with Destabilizing Rounds / Repulsor Brace** — consumes Volatile Rounds, free overshields between Soul Siphon casts.
- **Void/Kinetic heavy** for damage phases (Void heavies get Volatile + weaken synergy).

## Stat Priority (Armor 3.0)
Six stats (Weapons, Health, Class, Grenade, Super, Melee), each to 200, three per piece.
**Super (150–200) > Class (~100) ≈ Grenade (~100) > Health (~70).**
- **Super maxed** = faster regen *and* more super damage at the top end → more frequent, harder double Nova Bombs.
- **Class** = Rift uptime + overshields, pairs with Soul Siphon's class return.
- **Grenade** = feeds Devour + Volatile.
- **Health** = survival floor (death resets the loop).

## Artifact — NPA Repulsion Regulator *(re-evaluated)*

This is a native-Void Devour engine that lives on a board of Weakened + Volatile adds, generates a flood of Orbs (Siphons/Reaper/Bad Juju), and refunds Super off kills under Devour. NPA Repulsion Regulator is the only artifact in the pool that closes ALL of that loop's open ends at once, and it does so through mechanic interaction, not element matching. It supplies (1) a self-feeding Void Breach economy — Unto the Breach makes a Breach every time you kill a debuffed target (your entire board), Supernova turns each Breach pickup into a large weakening pulse that re-debuffs the next pack, and Protective Breach converts those Breaches into overshields that stack with Soul Siphon for near-unkillability; (2) native Overload Champion coverage via Overload Grenades on the Vortex grenade you already throw every loop — critical because the doc explicitly warns Champion coverage is now frame-gated in 9.7.0; (3) Volatile Flow turning your constant Orbs into Volatile Rounds, which feeds Echo of Instability → Devour → grenade energy without spending the grenade; and (4) Void Weapon Channeling, a real Void-weapon damage multiplier whenever your Void abilities are charged — exactly the boss-DPS window where you pre-cast Rift and hold grenade/Soul Siphon. Encrypted Data Disk (the doc's current pick) only offers Power from Pain as a second Devour source plus generic sniper filler; its Void Infestation weaken-spread is nice but NPA matches that intent through Supernova+Unto the Breach AND adds overshield generation, native champion stun, and a DPS multiplier Encrypted Data Disk lacks.

## Gameplay Loop
1. Drop **Rift** (Bomber kicks back grenade energy; Insulation Orbs + Soul Siphon rebuild the next Rift).
2. **Vortex Grenade** into a pack → Volatile Rounds + Weaken.
3. **Clear with Bad Juju** → String of Curses stacks, Orbs spawn, Devour procs, weapon kills under Devour return super.
4. **Pocket Singularity → Soul Siphon** for Rift energy + overshield.
5. Super up → **Cataclysm into the densest pack.**
6. **Free Lance into the Cataclysm** → triple-detonation.
7. Mass Nova Bomb final blows → Skull + Reprisal dump super back; Orbs feed mods.
8. Repeat. ~15s super in dense content.

## Boss DPS Rotation
1. Pre-cast **Empowering Rift** at the boss's feet.
2. **Weaken** the boss (Vortex Grenade via Echo of Undermining; Pocket Singularity adds Volatile).
3. **Cataclysm** at the boss.
4. **Lance into it point-blank** → all three blasts land in one window (huge burst).
5. Empty **Void heavy** in the Empowering Rift while weakened.
6. Spam Bad Juju + grenades to rebuild super; boss-room adds refund fast.
> Against a lone boss with **no adds**, refund stalls — bring it adds / use in encounters that spawn them.

## Strengths / Weaknesses
**Strengths:** Elite super uptime; one of the highest burst single-target windows (triple-detonation in one cast); excellent add-clear; near-unkillable (Devour + Soul Siphon overshields + chest resist mods); closed Orb-to-super economy.
**Weaknesses:** Refund is **kill-gated** (stalls vs lone bosses); death resets Devour; base Voidwalker only; depends on Bad Juju + Renegades artifact.
