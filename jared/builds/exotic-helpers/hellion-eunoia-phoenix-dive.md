## Why Solar (not Prismatic)
Hellion is a Solar aspect and its whole payoff (Consul's Pitch, scorch, ignitions, Firesprites) lives in the Solar ecosystem. **Solar lets you run Hellion + Touch of Flame** (Touch of Flame is Solar-only and the single best Hellion amplifier) — Prismatic *can't* pair them. Solar also opens the full **Ember fragment suite** that accelerates the scorch loop, and Phoenix Dive's cure-on-landing gives survivability without a Healing Rift. Prismatic is only better if you specifically want Devour/Transcendence.

## Abilities
- **Super:** **Song of Flame** — weapons Radiant, abilities supercharged, **Hellion fires faster + grenades chain harder**; pairs with the artifact's Heart of the Flame. (Daybreak = roaming-DPS alt.)
- **Class Ability:** **Phoenix Dive** (replaces the Rift). **Casting it summons/re-summons Hellion** — the activation trigger. Landing **cures you** (survivability instead of Healing Rift). Class>100 still grants an **overshield on every dive**. Loop self-sustains via Ember of Singeing (scorching targets speeds class ability recharge) plus Focusing Strike and Absolution orbs, so it returns in ~10–15s.
- **Grenade:** **Fusion Grenade** — with Touch of Flame **detonates twice**, heavy scorch → fast ignitions/Firesprites.
- **Melee:** **Incinerator Snap** — snap a fan of burning projectiles for ranged scorch.

### Aspects
1. **Hellion** *(required)* — Phoenix Dive summons a Solar mortar turret that scorches distant targets.
2. **Touch of Flame** — upgrades Solar grenades/abilities (Fusion double-detonates, etc.). Best Hellion partner — accelerates scorch→ignite→Firesprite→Phoenix Dive. Solar-exclusive (the main reason to stay off Prismatic).

### Fragments (Solar Ember suite)
- **Ember of Ashes** — more scorch per hit (reach ignition faster; compounds with Consul's Pitch shrapnel).
- **Ember of Char** — ignitions spread scorch + **create Firesprites** (core Phoenix Dive fuel).
- **Ember of Singeing** — applying Scorch to a target speeds class ability (Phoenix Dive) recharge (perfect for dive-to-summon).
- **Ember of Eruption** — bigger ignition radius/damage.
- *(5th if available)* **Ember of Empyrean** — Solar/ignition kills extend Restoration & Radiant (sustain without a rift).

> Locked four: **Ashes + Char + Singeing + Eruption.**

## Exotic — Eunoia (*Consul's Pitch*)
> *Hellion projectiles deal up to **+20% damage** after traveling **17m**. Projectiles in flight for **1s** release **4 tracking Shrapnel**, each dealing Solar damage and applying **5 stacks of Scorch.***

Up to **20 scorch per mortar** spread across targets → constant ignitions + Firesprites → the exact fuel Phoenix Dive needs. Converts Hellion from chip-damage buddy into a self-perpetuating room-clearing scorch engine. (Now auto-Tier 5.)

## Weapons
- **Solar Wave-Frame GL (Demolitionist + Incandescent)** — your area-denial GL: Incandescent spreads Scorch (feeding ignitions/Firesprites) while Demolitionist refunds grenade energy. Excellent legendary fit for this build's Solar scorch/grenade economy.
- **Polaris Lance** (Exotic Solar Scout) — Perfect Fifth self-igniting explosions layer more scorch/ignitions.
- **Sunshot / Incandescent Solar primary** — chains explosions, spreads scorch for Firesprites. *(Devil's Ruin is a fun Firesprite-fed alt.)*
- **Solar Heavy** (Fixed Odds MG / Solar GL) — sustained add-clear + boss DPS during Song of Flame.
- Keep weapons **Solar** to stack Solar Surge + Radiant. Pick frames to cover champions (anti-champ is frame-intrinsic).

## Stat Priority (Armor 3.0)
**Class ~120–130 > Grenade 100+ > Super 100+ > Health > Weapons > Melee.**
- **Class** — shorter Phoenix Dive + **overshield over 100** (high value; no rift to fall back on).
- **Grenade** — Fusion/scorch uptime (Firesprites double-dip refund).
- **Super** — Song of Flame faster + over-100 super damage.
> 125% cooldown cap means uptime comes from **Firesprites + Solar kills + mods**, not raw Class stacking — hit the ~110–130 overshield breakpoint, rest into Grenade/Super.

## Artifact — Queensfoil Censer *(re-evaluated)*

Of the 7 pools, Queensfoil Censer is the only one whose perk cluster mechanically chains with this build's actual engine: Radiant -> Scorch -> Ignition -> Firesprite -> Phoenix Dive. The decisive interactions are Fever and Chill (Solar precision hits self-generate Radiant), Kindling Trigger (Radiant makes Solar weapons apply Scorch on hit), and Rays of Precision (Radiant Solar precision kills auto-ignite) — three perks that turn every Solar shot into a scorch/ignition source that stacks on top of Eunoia's shrapnel (4 shrapnel x5 scorch per mortar), accelerating ignitions and Firesprite spawns, which is exactly what refunds Phoenix Dive (and thus re-summons Hellion). Heart of the Flame then weaponizes the Song of Flame window. No other artifact has a Solar-radiant-scorch backbone: Hunter's Journal and Implement of Curiosity each have only 1-2 scattered Solar perks (Radiant Orbs, Solar Fulmination, Fever and Chill, Radiant Shrapnel) buried in Stasis/Strand/Void pools, and NPA, Encrypted Data Disk, Slayer Baron's and Tablet of Ruin have effectively zero Solar-ability synergy. This is a mechanic match, not just an element match.

## Gameplay Loop
1. **Phoenix Dive** → summons/refreshes **Hellion** (Powerful Attraction grabs sprites/orbs; Bomber/Dynamo refund).
2. **Hellion fires long-range mortars** → Consul's Pitch ramps +20% past 17m and **fractures into 4 shrapnel × 5 scorch** — blankets the pack.
3. **Add scorch** with Fusion (double-det), Incinerator Snap, Solar weapons.
4. **Scorch → Ignitions** (Eruption/Char) clear groups + spawn **Firesprites**.
5. **Grab Firesprites** → refund grenade + Phoenix Dive (~10% each).
6. **Phoenix Dive returns ~10–15s** (Singeing + Focusing Strike + Absolution) → re-summon Hellion → repeat.
7. **Song of Flame** when charged → Radiant weapons, supercharged Hellion/grenades, team buff (Heart of the Flame) → burst window. The dive *is* the engine — no rift.

## Strengths / Weaknesses
**Strengths:** Near-permanent Hellion uptime (turret auto-clears); excellent ranged scorch/ignition AoE for dense endgame; self-sustaining economy + survivability **without a Healing Rift** (overshield + cure-on-dive + Restoration); **no rift-swapping** by design.
**Weaknesses:** Modest boss/single-target DPS (leans on Song of Flame windows + Solar heavy); Hellion is range-dependent (wants 17m+, weaker in tight rooms); must actively time dives for healing; Firesprite-dependent (kills dry up → uptime dips).
