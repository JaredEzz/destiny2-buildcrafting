## Exotic — Mataiodoxia (Exotic Chest)
Intrinsic 'Stylostixis': targets damaged by Arcane Needle emit a suspending detonation when defeated; hitting a marked/needle-stuck target with a second Arcane Needle instantly suspends it. Defeating suspended targets returns melee energy, creating a self-sustaining Arcane Needle → suspend → melee-energy loop. Needles pierce shields including Barrier Champion shields (1 needle in low/mid content, ~2 in GM-tier). In 9.7.0 the Needlestorm Super also triggers the Stylostixis suspending burst (5 projectiles on a target) for a large AoE damage spike.

## Abilities
- **Super:** Needlestorm — buffed in Update 9.7.0 so that hitting a target with 5 Super projectiles also triggers the Mataiodoxia Stylostixis suspending burst; supercombine burst damage up ~22%, now roughly equal to Cuirass Thundercrash as a burst nuke while still seeding a Threadling army on add-dense encounters.
- **Class Ability:** Healing Rift (committed — steady healing to anchor the threadling/suspend playstyle; no rift-swapping. Woven Mail comes from Thread of Warding Orb pickups, not the Rift itself)
- **Grenade:** Shackle Grenade (suspends a cluster on impact; consumed by Mindspun Invocation to activate Weaver's Trance). Threadling Grenade is the alternate when you want max Threadling output instead of on-demand suspend.
- **Melee:** Arcane Needle (3 charges) — the core of the build; pierces shields (incl. Barrier), Unravels, marks targets, and via Mataiodoxia creates suspending detonations.

### Aspects
- Mindspun Invocation (consume Shackle Grenade for Weaver's Trance — Final Blows create suspending detonations; enhances Grapple/Shackle/Threadling grenades, and now spawns a Threadling Nest on hold-grenade in 9.7.0)
- The Wanderer (thrown Tangles attach to targets and detonate in a suspending burst; Threadling final blows create Tangles — turns your Tangle economy into AoE crowd control)

### Fragments
- Thread of Warding (picking up an Orb of Power grants Woven Mail — survivability)
- Thread of Continuity (extends Suspend, Sever, and Unravel duration — central to a suspend-lock build)
- Thread of Generation (dealing damage generates grenade energy — refuels Shackle for Weaver's Trance)
- Thread of Fury (damaging targets with a Tangle grants melee energy — feeds Arcane Needle alongside suspend kills)

## Weapons
- **Lost Signal** (Legendary Stasis Area-Denial GL) — lingering damage pools for relentless passive add-clear. It's a Legendary, so it pairs fine with Monte Carlo — but note it's Stasis, so its kills don't feed your Tangle/Threadling/Unravel economy; slot it for zone control, not Strand synergy.
- Monte Carlo (Exotic Kinetic AR) — best-in-slot here: sustained damage returns melee energy to refill Arcane Needle and its catalyst gives a free powered melee, supercharging the suspend loop and Transcendence on Prismatic variants
- Legendary Strand Submachine Gun or Auto with Hatchling/Unraveling Rounds to spawn Threadlings and Unravel for Tangle generation
- **The Call** (legendary Strand Rocket-Assisted Sidearm) — the best legendary fit: **Hatchling** spawns Threadlings and **Slice** applies Sever, both feeding the suspend/Threadling/Tangle economy. Keep Monte Carlo for the melee loop, or run The Call as your damage primary if you prefer it
- A Strand Rocket / Heavy with Unstoppable-frame intrinsic (9.7.0 anti-champion is now weapon-frame intrinsic) for boss DPS
- Any Wave/Aggressive/Heavy-Burst-frame heavy for intrinsic Unstoppable, or a Precision/Adaptive frame for intrinsic Barrier — match frames to the Champions present since champion stuns no longer come from the artifact

## Stat Priority
Armor 3.0 priority: Class (Recovery, to 100 first for fast Rift + energy gain; push toward 100+ for class-cast overshield) → Melee (drives Arcane Needle uptime and Mataiodoxia loop; aim 70+, higher if you want needle as primary damage) → Grenade (~70, fuels Shackle/Weaver's Trance) → Health (survivability buffer) → Super → Weapons. Target archetype: Reaver (Class/Melee) or Demolitionist (Grenade/Class) armor pieces; Ghost Armorer mod to bias drops.

## Artifact — Tablet of Ruin *(re-evaluated)*

Not picked for "Strand element match" but because it is the only one of the 7 artifacts that closes this build's entire verb chain. Mataiodoxia's loop is Arcane Needle (Unravel + mark) -> suspend -> Tangle/Threadling spam -> Needlestorm. Tablet of Ruin has a perk for every link: Unraveling Orbs converts the Orbs (Thread of Warding) and the constant Tangles (The Wanderer + Thread of Fury) into weapon Unravel; Horde Shuttle turns that Unravel into auto-spawned Threadlings that themselves Sever; Vile Weave makes thrown Tangles apply Sever and cuts Tangle cooldown; To Shreds turns Severed kills back into Strand hotspots/Tangles and Unravel, looping into Unraveling Orbs again; and Elemental Supercharger feeds Needlestorm (Strand = Super element) off Severed/Exhausted kills so the 9.7.0 Stylostixis burst comes around far faster. No other artifact touches Strand verbs at all — Implement of Curiosity has Threaded Blast/Tangled Web/Pack Tactics but lacks the Super-feed and the Sever->Tangle recursion, and Hunter's Journal only has scattered Threaded Blast/Threaded-adjacent perks. Tablet is the engine; the others are loose parts.

## Gameplay Loop
1) Cast Healing Rift on contact for sustain, and grab Orbs as you fight — Thread of Warding turns them into Woven Mail. 2) Throw Arcane Needle into a pack to Unravel/mark; a second needle into a marked target instantly suspends (or kill the marked target to trigger the Stylostixis suspending detonation). 3) Consume Shackle Grenade via Mindspun Invocation to enter Weaver's Trance — now your weapon/ability Final Blows create suspending detonations for ~25s. 4) Use Monte Carlo to mow suspended adds; sustained damage + Thread of Fury Tangles refund melee energy, so Arcane Needle stays nearly permanently charged. 5) Strand kills drop Tangles — throw or shoot them; The Wanderer turns each into another suspending detonation, chaining lockdown across the room. 6) Thread of Generation rebuilds grenade energy from damage to re-arm Shackle. 7) Cast Needlestorm on a boss or elite cluster: 5 projectiles into the target trigger the buffed Stylostixis burst (~Cuirass Thundercrash damage) while the rest re-weave into a Threadling swarm for cleanup.

## Strengths
Elite crowd control — near-permanent room-wide Suspend that locks down Champions and majors (Arcane Needle pierces Barrier shields intrinsically, so it doubles as champion handling). Self-sustaining ability economy (suspend kills + Tangles refund melee; Monte Carlo refills needles) means abilities almost never go dry. Strong survivability from Woven Mail (Thread of Warding Orb pickups) plus Healing Rift. The 9.7.0 Needlestorm buff adds real single-target burst (Cuirass-tier), so it now contributes to DPS instead of being add-clear only. Scales cleanly into Master/GM Nightfalls, raids, dungeons, and Onslaught.

## Weaknesses
Burst boss DPS outside the Super window is mediocre — it leans on suspend/CC and Threadlings, not raw damage, so it underperforms dedicated DPS builds in short damage phases. Heavily reliant on the Arcane Needle loop; if you miss needles or get melee-locked, the engine stalls. Suspend has diminishing value against many raid/encounter bosses that are immune to Suspend. Threadling pathing can waste damage on cover-heavy arenas. Requires hitting Class and Melee stat breakpoints to feel smooth.
