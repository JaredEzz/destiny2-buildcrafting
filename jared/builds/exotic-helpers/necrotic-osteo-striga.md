> **Two accuracy notes:** Osteo Striga is a **Kinetic** Weapon of Sorrow SMG (not Strand) — the Strand part is the subclass, and Necrotic Grip is what makes its poison *chain*. **Thread of Ascent** is a grenade-triggered reload/handling/AE fragment (NOT a damage fragment) — it's an optional QoL swap, not a core piece.

## Why Strand (not Prismatic)
1. **Suspend replaces lost ability uptime.** With the 125% cooldown cap, Broodweaver's **Mindspun → Weaver's Trance** turns every final blow into a suspending detonation — the poison chain crowd-controls *for free* without burning ability energy.
2. **The poison chain feeds the subclass loop:** Osteo/Necrotic kills → Tangles → re-suspend → suspended kills return class + grenade energy. Closed loop.
3. **Weavewalk** (Strand-exclusive) is the best panic button for a stand-your-ground poison-tank — note it's an *optional aspect swap* (take it over The Wanderer); this build's default pair is Mindspun + The Wanderer.
> *Prismatic alt:* Feed the Void/Devour gives full heal + grenade on poison kills, but you still need the real Necrotic Grip (Spirit-of class item loses the poison *scaling*), and it has no suspend. Take it only if you want Devour healing over suspend lockdown.

## Abilities
- **Super:** **Needlestorm** — needle barrage → Threadlings (whose kills feed innate Tangle generation); a room-reset, not a DPS rotation (boss-super gen is cut 60%).
- **Class Ability:** **Healing Rift** (committed) — anchor a chokepoint and tank while poison + suspend do the work. Empowering is wasted here (your kills are DoT poison ticks, not weapon DPS).
- **Grenade:** **Shackle Grenade** — instant suspend + the grenade you *consume* for Weaver's Trance.
- **Melee:** **Arcane Needle** — applies Unravel; **with Necrotic Grip, melee final blows seed poison that spreads on death.**

### Aspects
1. **Mindspun Invocation** — consume Shackle → ~25s **Weaver's Trance** (all final blows make suspending detonations). The suspended kills then spawn Tangles via innate Strand kill-generation and refund class energy via Thread of Mind.
2. **The Wanderer** — thrown Tangles attach and detonate into suspend. (Tangle *generation* itself is innate to Strand kills — your poison/Threadling kills keep it fed.) *(Chosen over Weaver's Call for suspend control over Threadling spam.)*

### Fragments
- **Thread of Continuity** — extends Suspend/Unravel/Sever duration.
- **Thread of Generation** — damage → grenade energy (Osteo's many hits refill Shackle fast → keeps Weaver's Trance up).
- **Thread of Mind** — suspended-target kills → class energy (refunds Healing Rift).
- **Thread of Binding** — Super final blows emit a suspending burst (amplifies Needlestorm's room-reset).
> *Thread of Ascent* is a QoL swap (auto-reload Osteo on grenade + AE/handling) over Thread of Binding only if reload uptime feels bad — it adds **no** damage.

## Exotic — Necrotic Grip
Melee/poison final blows spread **Necrotic poison** to nearby targets, and poison damage **scales as the chain runs**. Grants bonus AE to Weapons of Sorrow and — with Osteo — **rides the spreading poison on every bullet**, so any tagged enemy that dies passes poison onward. **MoT:** now **Tier 5** (full tuning access) — higher Health/Class floor improves the tank profile; the poison-spread mechanic was **not** nerfed.

## Weapons
- **Lost Signal** (Exotic Strand Area-Denial GL) — bouncing area-denial add-clear that feeds your Tangle/Unravel economy. Exotic, so run it *instead of* Osteo when you want pure area denial over the poison chain.
- **Osteo Striga** (Exotic Kinetic SMG) — *Toxic Overload:* sustained fire / final blows release a seeking **poison burst**; with Necrotic Grip the spreading poison rides every bullet → **endless room-clearing chain.** The centerpiece.
- **The Call** (legendary Strand Rocket-Assisted Sidearm) — recommended energy slot: **Hatchling** spawns Threadlings (→ Tangles → suspend), **Slice** applies Sever, and it patches Osteo's weakness by providing single-target/ranged **starter kills** to ignite the chain.
- **Heavy** — a Strand/Void MG or Linear for boss/champion DPS. Anti-champ is frame-intrinsic now, so just bring the right frame.

## Stat Priority (Armor 3.0)
**Health > Class > Grenade > Weapons > Super > Melee.**
- **Health** — survivability anchor (over-100 DR scaling = the poison-tank identity).
- **Class** — Healing Rift uptime.
- **Grenade** — Shackle = Weaver's Trance fuel (base stat matters under the cooldown cap).
> Archetype: **Siegebreaker (Health/Grenade)** or **Reaver (Class/Melee)**.

## Gameplay Loop
1. **Healing Rift** on a chokepoint — you're tanking.
2. **Shackle Grenade** into a pack → suspends + (Mindspun) grants **Weaver's Trance** (~25s).
3. **Osteo Striga** into the group — Necrotic rides poison on every bullet.
4. First death → **poison spreads** → neighbors die → spreads again; under Weaver's Trance each death also **suspends**.
5. Suspended kills spawn **Tangles** (innate Strand generation) + return class energy (Thread of Mind).
6. **Throw a Tangle** at the next room → re-suspend → re-seed with Osteo/The Call → loop restarts.
7. Top off Shackle from weapon damage (Thread of Generation); if pushed, retreat into your **Healing Rift** behind a fresh suspend; **Needlestorm** to hard-reset a swarm.

## Strengths / Weaknesses
**Strengths:** Among the best raw add-clear in the game; near-constant suspend lockdown; double survival stack (Healing Rift + Woven Mail via Shield Crush) that self-feeds through kills (thrives under MoT's cooldown nerfs); Tier 5 Necrotic Grip + intrinsic anti-champ give more stat headroom and loadout freedom than ever.
**Weaknesses:** Low single-target/boss DPS (poison is add-clear; bring a real heavy); the chain needs a **starter kill** to ignite (The Call patches this); close-range identity = melee danger if the chain breaks.
