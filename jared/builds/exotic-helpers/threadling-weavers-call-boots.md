## Why Strand (not Prismatic)
Weaver's Call and Boots of the Assembler both trigger on **Rift cast**, but have different needs: **Weaver's Call must be slotted as an aspect**, while **Boots is subclass-agnostic** (Noble Seekers spawn from *any* Rift). **Base Strand (Broodweaver) is the only place this full loop coexists** — Prismatic does carry Weaver's Call, but **Mindspun Invocation is NOT in the Prismatic Warlock aspect pool** (Lightning Surge, Hellion, Feed the Void, Bleak Watcher, Weaver's Call only), and Prismatic takes Facets, not the Thread fragments this swarm runs on. Strand gives you Weaver's Call + Mindspun together, the full Thread pool, and Needlestorm natively.

> **Boots is NOT Solar-locked.** Its Healing-Rift Noble Seekers + rift-extension work fully on Strand; the classic "Noble Rounds" weapon role is filled by **Service of Luzaku** instead of Lumina.

## Abilities
- **Super:** Needlestorm — heavy damage and **leaves Threadlings** on impact (feeds the swarm).
- **Class Ability:** **Healing Rift** (committed) — the trigger for the entire loop (Weaver's Call volley + Boots' Noble Seekers on one cast). Stand-your-ground survival via Healing Rift + Woven Mail + Luzaku, not Empowering burst.
- **Grenade:** Threadling Grenade — **Mindspun Invocation now turns the charged throw into a roaming Threadling Nest turret** (3rd swarm source).
- **Melee:** Arcane Needle — 3 charges, **unravels** its target + Strand damage/kills that perch more Threadlings for Weaver's Call.

### Aspects
1. **Weaver's Call** *(required)* — Rift cast weaves 3 Threadlings + deploys all perched ones (up to 8); Strand kills perch more.
2. **Mindspun Invocation** — extra fragment slots + upgrades Threadling Grenade into a roaming **Nest turret**. *(Alt: The Wanderer for suspending Tangles, but Mindspun's turret is the stronger swarm multiplier and Healing Rift + Luzaku already cover survival.)*

### Fragments
- **Thread of Rebirth** — Strand weapon kills can make a Threadling (Luzaku turns every kill into more swarm — core).
- **Thread of Warding** — Orb pickup → **Woven Mail** (primary DR layer).
- **Thread of Generation** — damage → grenade energy (cycles the nest).
- **Thread of Evolution** — Threadlings travel farther and hit harder (buffs every swarm source).
- **Thread of Continuity** — Unravel/Sever last longer (stretches Arcane Needle + Slice uptime).

## Weapons
- **Lost Signal** (legendary Stasis Area-Denial GL) — bouncing area-denial projectiles for relentless zone control. It's a legendary, so it coexists fine with Luzaku — but note it's Stasis, so its kills don't feed the Threadling/Unravel economy; slot it purely for area denial.
- **Service of Luzaku** (Exotic Strand LMG — the heal-MG you wanted) — *Dichotomous Thinking:* hip-fire allies while charged heals them, refills mag from reserves, **spawns Threadlings from allies**, and ramps weapon damage. *Meaning-Making:* final blows create a **Threadling Nest**. The swarm's sustain engine.
- **The Call** (legendary Strand Rocket-Assisted Sidearm) — top legendary pick: **Hatchling** final blows spawn Threadlings (stacks with Weaver's Call + Thread of Rebirth + Luzaku for an absurd swarm) and **Slice** applies Sever. Or any Strand SMG/auto to proc Thread of Rebirth — pick a *frame* that covers a champion type (anti-champ is frame-intrinsic now).
- **Special** (Strand glaive / strong special) — pick its frame to cover your second champion type. *(Luzaku takes the heavy slot, so cover both champ types across primary + special.)*

## Stat Priority (Armor 3.0)
**Class ~110–125 > Health 100+ > Grenade ~80 > Weapons (over-100 secondary) > Super/Melee (dump).**
- **Class** — Rift is the whole loop trigger; push toward the 125% cap to re-cast constantly.
- **Health** — shield cap + max HP reinforce the face-tank identity.
- **Grenade** — keeps the Threadling Grenade/nest cycling.

## Artifact — Implement of Curiosity *(re-evaluated)*

This is the threadling/Strand artifact, and it is a categorical upgrade over the build's original Encrypted Data Disk (a Kinetic/Void/Arc Swords/Snipers/MG artifact with essentially zero Threadling, Unravel, or Woven-Mail synergy — it was picked on a wrong 'Strand-loop' premise). Implement of Curiosity directly multiplies every pillar of this build: (1) Horde Shuttle spawns EXTRA Threadlings from damaging unraveled targets — a 4th swarm source stacked on Weaver's Call + Mindspun nest + Luzaku nests, and those bonus Threadlings themselves apply Sever, deepening the Darkness-debuff field; (2) Pack Tactics ramps Threadling damage as they fight and at max stacks lets Threadlings stun Unstoppable Champions — this single perk solves the build's biggest hole (the swarm having no anti-champ), turning hands-off add-clear into hands-off champ stunning; (3) Refresh Threads turns Strand pickups/Tangles into least-charged-ability energy, shortening the Healing-Rift cooldown that triggers the entire one-button loop; (4) Elemental Benevolence converts the build's constant ally-buffing (Boots' Noble Seekers heals, Woven Mail sharing) into class-ability energy — Rift literally refunds itself when you support the team. No other artifact in the pool touches Threadling generation, Threadling-based champ stuns, and Strand-pickup Rift regen simultaneously.

## Gameplay Loop
1. **Service of Luzaku** kills → plant a **Threadling Nest** (or hip-fire allies to spawn threadlings from them).
2. **Cast Healing Rift** → one button = Weaver's Call volley + Boots' Noble Seekers (heal team + extend rift) + loop start.
3. **Charged Threadling Grenade** → Mindspun roaming **Nest turret** (now 3 autonomous swarm sources).
4. **Anchor in the rift, feed the swarm** with Luzaku — kills near nests + Thread of Rebirth spawn more threadlings; heal-bursts top off allies + the mag.
5. **Pick up Orbs** → Woven Mail + Recuperation heal + class/grenade energy → **re-cast Rift almost immediately** (Bomber/Outreach + Insulation orb energy).
6. **Needlestorm** for boss damage / room reset — leaves more Threadlings, looping to step 2.

## Strengths / Weaknesses
**Strengths:** Elite survivability (Healing Rift + Woven Mail + Luzaku + Recuperation orbs); massive hands-off add-clear from three threadling sources; strong fireteam support (Noble Seekers + heal-MG); low ammo stress (Luzaku refills from reserves).
**Weaknesses:** Modest single-target/boss DPS (threadlings are add-clear; boss leans on Needlestorm windows + special); ramp-up time before the swarm peaks; part of Boots' value (ally healing) is co-op-weighted, muted in true solo.
