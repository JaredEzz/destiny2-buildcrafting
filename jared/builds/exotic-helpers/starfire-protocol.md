## Exotic — Starfire Protocol (chest)
Grants a second Fusion Grenade charge. The reworked Fusion Harness perk now triggers on weapon HITS while Radiant (no longer requires Empowering Rift specifically), and works inside Empowering Rift and Well of Radiance: +5% grenade energy per empowered weapon hit and +15% instantly on empowered weapon final blows. Defeating a target with a Fusion Grenade fully recharges your Rift. With Touch of Flame doubling Fusion Grenade detonations, this creates near-infinite mini-nuke grenade spam that also one-shots Champions ~25 power under and chunks bosses during DPS phases.

## Abilities
- **Super:** Well of Radiance (team healing + Radiant + boss-DPS damage buff). Swap to Song of Flame on Prismatic-flex days, but on pure Solar this build runs Well of Radiance for the empowering-rift damage zone and to keep Fusion Harness active for the whole fireteam.
- **Class Ability:** Empowering Rift (committed — Fusion Harness procs from weapon hits while Radiant, so grenade regen never depends on swapping rifts; Heat Rises' Cure on grenade consume and Ember of Resolve cover heals between casts).
- **Grenade:** Fusion Grenade
- **Melee:** Incinerator Snap (Scorch application, feeds ignitions and Ember of Searing/Singeing for ability energy)

### Aspects
- Touch of Flame (Fusion Grenades detonate twice, doubling damage and Scorch — the core damage multiplier)
- Heat Rises (consume your grenade to gain Cure and extended airborne benefits — the on-demand heal that enables grenade-spam-while-healing). Icarus Dash is the alternative second aspect for add-clear mobility if you do not need the Heat Rises healing loop.

### Fragments
- Ember of Ashes (increased Scorch stacks applied — more/faster ignitions)
- Ember of Singeing (your class ability recharges faster when you Scorch targets — rift economy)
- Ember of Torches (powered melee against combatants makes you Radiant)
- Ember of Searing (defeating Scorched targets grants melee energy + spawns a Firesprite, which restores grenade energy on pickup)
- Ember of Resolve (grenade final blows cure you) or Ember of Empyrean (Solar weapon/ability kills extend Restoration/Radiant) — swap in for one of the above on harder content (four fragment slots total with Touch of Flame + Heat Rises)

## Weapons
- **Solar Wave-Frame GL (Demolitionist + Incandescent)** — your area-denial GL: Incandescent spreads Scorch (feeding ignitions/Firesprites) while Demolitionist refunds grenade energy. Excellent legendary fit for this build's Solar scorch/grenade economy.
- Polaris Lance (Exotic Solar Scout) — meta resurgence in Monument of Triumph, triggers a huge number of ignitions and keeps you at range; strong with Solar artifact synergy
- Sunshot (Exotic Solar Hand Cannon) — explosive add-clear that procs Scorch/Radiant chains
- Zaouli's Bane (Legendary Solar Hand Cannon, Explosive Payload + Incandescent/Demolitionist) — best-in-slot Solar primary for grenade refund + Scorch spread
- Special: a Solar Fusion Rifle or Solar Shotgun for burst, OR a strong Solar Special for the Fusion Harness weapon-hit loop
- Heavy: Solar Machine Gun or Solar Rocket (e.g. The Hothead / a Solar LMG) to abuse Solar Surge x3 during Well; intrinsic anti-Champion frames now cover champs (Anti-Champion 2.0)

## Stat Priority
Grenade (primary, push toward 100-130 for fastest Fusion cooldown and chunk scaling) > Class (100+ for overshield-on-rift and faster rift/energy economy) > Super (Well uptime) > Health (~70 for survivability floor) > Weapons > Melee (~70 floor). Six-stat Armor 3.0 system — tiers are gone, every point counts; target an archetype like Demolitionist (Grenade/Class) or Siegebreaker (Health/Grenade) when farming armor.

## Artifact — Queensfoil Censer *(re-evaluated)*

It is the only artifact in the pool that simultaneously generates Radiant, applies Scorch, and converts precision kills into Ignitions on a pure Solar subclass - and Radiant is this build's single point of failure (Fusion Harness only refunds grenade energy on EMPOWERED/Radiant weapon hits, and Touch-of-Flame nukes are only worth firing between if you can keep grenades charged). Fever and Chill (T1) hands you Radiant from weapon precision hits, removing the build's stated weakness of depending on landing powered Incinerator Snaps. Kindling Trigger (T1) then makes those same Radiant weapons paint Scorch on every target, and Rays of Precision (T3) detonates Ignitions on precision kills - so your primary becomes a self-sustaining scorch/ignition machine that runs in parallel with grenade spam. No competing artifact (Implement of Curiosity has Radiant Shrapnel/Cauterized Darkness but no native Radiant generator at the right tiers; Slayer Baron is Stasis/GL-centric) packages Radiant-gen + scorch + ignition + an orb/Armor-Charge economy for Well casting the way Queensfoil does. Note: the build markdown mis-describes Torch and Heart of the Flame as scorch/sustain perks; the authoritative datamine shows Torch is a conditional Strand/Stasis-debuff damage buff and Heart of the Flame is a Super-cast support perk - so the real Solar engine is Fever and Chill + Kindling Trigger + Rays of Precision, which Queensfoil owns.

## Gameplay Loop
1) Open by getting Radiant: use a powered Incinerator Snap melee with Ember of Torches. 2) Cast Empowering Rift (or Well of Radiance for a damage phase) and start throwing Fusion Grenades — Touch of Flame makes each detonate twice for nuke-level damage. 3) While Radiant inside the rift, every empowered weapon HIT refunds +5% grenade (and final blows +15%), so you fire your Solar primary between throws to keep grenades charged. 4) Fusion Grenade kills fully refund your Rift; recast for a fresh empowering zone. 5) Consume a grenade with Heat Rises to gain Cure when you need to reposition or heal, then immediately resume. 6) On boss phases: drop Well of Radiance, stack Solar Surge x3, and alternate double-detonation Fusion Grenades with Heavy weapon damage. Kindling Trigger + Rays of Precision keep ignitions chaining through adds the entire time.

## Strengths
Elite ability uptime — effectively infinite Fusion Grenade spam that one-shots Champions ~25 power under and delivers excellent sustained boss DPS during Well. Doubles as top-tier add-clear via ignitions/Scorch chains. Built-in survivability with Cure (Heat Rises grenade consume, Ember of Resolve), Restoration from Heart of the Flame ability kills, and Class 100+ overshield. Brings a team buff (Well + Radiant + Fusion Harness sharing). Anti-Champion is now intrinsic to weapon frames, so no artifact slots are wasted on champ mods.

## Weaknesses
Damage is bottlenecked by Radiant uptime — if you can't keep landing powered Incinerator Snaps (Ember of Torches) or sitting in Well, the grenade engine slows. Empowering Rift / Well anchor you to a spot, which is risky against high roaming damage in GMs. The ~125% active-cooldown cap and ability-economy nerfs in 9.7.0 mean you must lean on Grenade stat + weapon-hit refunds rather than pure cooldown stacking. Lower raw single-target burst than the new Skull of Dire Ahamkara double-Nova-Bomb Void build for one-and-done DPS checks. Reliant on a Solar special/heavy uptime for the Fusion Harness loop.
