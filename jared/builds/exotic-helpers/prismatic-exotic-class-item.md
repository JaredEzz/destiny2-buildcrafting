## Exotic — Solipsism (Exotic Class Item) — roll: Spirit of Apotheosis + Spirit of the Star-Eater
Spirit of the Star-Eater: collecting Orbs of Power while your Super is full overcharges it (up to ~70% / x4 bonus Super damage) AND Orbs grant bonus Super energy so you build Super faster. Spirit of Apotheosis: when your Super ends you gain ~10 seconds of drastically accelerated grenade and melee regen — letting you immediately spam Vortex grenades / Demolitionist rocket reloads right after the Nova Bomb burst. Together: charge Super fast, hoard Orbs to overcharge it, dump a massive Nova Bomb, then flood the encounter with abilities during the Apotheosis window.

## Abilities
- **Super:** Nova Bomb: Cataclysm (burst DPS) — swap to Song of Flame if your fireteam needs a buff/ad-clear super
- **Class Ability:** Healing Rift (committed — Devour + Void Overshield carry survival; no rift-swapping)
- **Grenade:** Vortex Grenade (pulls targets in with heavy lingering Void damage — your main Apotheosis-window spam)
- **Melee:** Arcane Needle (Strand melee — reliable Threadling/ability uptime) or Pocket Singularity for Void synergy

### Aspects
- Hellion (summons a Solar mortar turret on class ability cast — your Healing Rift; passive add-clear + Scorch, scales with Grenade stat)
- Feed the Void (Devour on ANY ability kill — strongest self-heal + grenade energy returns)

### Fragments
- Facet of Purpose (Orb pickup grants Void Overshield with Nova Bomb equipped; pair with the Powerful Attraction class item mod so your Rift cast vacuums Orbs — fuels Star-Eater)
- Facet of Dawn (powered melee hits grant Radiant, +25% weapon damage in PvE)
- Facet of Courage (Light abilities deal +damage to Darkness-debuffed targets — boosts Nova Bomb/Vortex/Hellion into ads unraveled by Arcane Needle)
- Facet of Protection (damage resist while surrounded — endgame survivability for GMs)

## Weapons
- Apex Predator / any Aggressive-frame rocket (intrinsic Unstoppable; Bait and Switch or Demolitionist for boss DPS dumped during Apotheosis window)
- Alternate Heavy: Tractor Cannon (debuff for fireteam burst windows on the Nova Bomb — run it in place of the rocket if a teammate covers heavy DPS)
- A high-Orb-generating primary with a Siphon mod or a Heavy-Burst/Precision frame (Barrier intrinsic) to keep Orbs flowing for Star-Eater
- Special: a Wave-frame GL (Unstoppable) for ad-clear; Alternate Heavy: Sleeper Simulant (Heavy slot) for sustained DPS between Supers in place of the rocket
- Legendary archetypes: Aggressive rocket, Rapid-Fire LMG, Precision/Adaptive primary — pick by which champion type the activity has, since anti-champ is now intrinsic by frame

## Stat Priority
Super → Class → Grenade (Armor 3.0): max Super for fastest Super loops (the build's whole point), Class (formerly Recovery) to ~100+ for fast Rifts and the 101+ overshield-on-cast, and Grenade to ~70+ since Hellion turret damage and Vortex output scale with the enhanced Grenade stat. Top off Health for GM survivability; Weapons/Melee are dump stats.

## Artifact — Queensfoil Censer *(re-evaluated)*

This is a Prismatic super-DPS chassis whose between-Super damage comes entirely from an Aggressive-frame heavy rocket (Apex Predator) and whose Super loop is fueled by hoarding Orbs of Power for Star-Eater. Queensfoil Censer is the only artifact in the pool with a real rocket-damage multiplier stack: Blast Radius (RL/GL final blows -> Armor Charge) feeds Argent Ordnance (firing a RL spends Armor Charge for a flat damage + reload buff), and Anti-Champion Nosecone adds RL bonus damage vs Champions. That trio directly patches the build's biggest stated weakness — mediocre sustained DPS between Supers. On top of that, Wished into Being spawns Orbs from ability final blows while the Super is nearly full, which is exactly the orb-overcharge window Star-Eater cares about, and Fever and Chill + Kindling Trigger give the Solar/radiant package that the Hellion-and-precision-primary half of the loop wants. IMPORTANT CORRECTION to the build doc: the doc justified Censer with Heart of the Flame ('Solar ability final blows refund energy') and Revitalizing Blast ('rocket final blows weaken + heal'). The real datamined perks are different — Heart of the Flame only triggers on casting a SOLAR Super (this build's Super is Void Nova Bomb, so it is dead weight) and Revitalizing Blast only ignites a stunned Champion and clears shield-stun. The artifact is still the correct pick, but for the rocket/orb package below, not the doc's reasons. The runner-up, NPA Repulsion Regulator, has cleaner Void-super synergy (Volatile Flow, Shatter Orbs, Supernova self-weaken, Protective Breach overshield) but no rocket-damage multiplier, so it loses on the heavy-DPS axis that defines this build.

## Gameplay Loop
1) Open by getting Devour up via any ability kill (Feed the Void) — this keeps you healed and refunds grenade energy all encounter. 2) Drop Hellion by casting your Healing Rift so the Solar turret passively clears adds and applies Scorch while you fight. 3) Generate Orbs of Power constantly (Siphon mods, grenade/melee kills, Firepower) — your Rift cast vacuums them via Powerful Attraction, and Facet of Purpose grants Void Overshield on pickup AND they feed Star-Eater + Super energy. 4) As Super charges, KEEP collecting Orbs after it's full to overcharge it (Star-Eater stacks, up to ~70%). 5) At the DPS window, apply a debuff (Tractor/Weaken via Revitalizing Blast), cast the overcharged Nova Bomb: Cataclysm for a massive burst. 6) The instant the Super ends, Spirit of Apotheosis gives ~10s of near-instant grenade/melee regen — chain Vortex grenades, melees, and reload-perk heavy (Demolitionist/Bait and Switch rockets) to extend the damage phase. 7) Repeat — Devour keeps you alive, Hellion keeps the room clear, Orbs keep the Super loop spinning.

## Strengths
Top-tier burst Super DPS that now competes with Hunters thanks to the Apotheosis post-Super ability dump; fully self-sufficient survivability via Devour + Void Overshield + Healing Rift; passive add-clear from Hellion; champion-agnostic in this update (anti-champ is intrinsic to weapon frames, so you just bring the right frames); flexible — same chassis pivots to Song of Flame for a support/ad-clear role.

## Weaknesses
Heavily Orb-dependent — if Orb generation dries up, both Star-Eater overcharge and Super uptime fall off hard; the big payoff is tied to Super/Apotheosis windows, so raw sustained DPS between Supers is only average without a strong heavy; Solipsism god roll (Apotheosis + Star-Eater) must be farmed; the new ability-economy nerfs (active cooldown cap cut to 125%) mean you lean on Apotheosis windows and Orbs rather than permanent grenade spam.
