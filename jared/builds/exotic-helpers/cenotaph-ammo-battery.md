## ⚡ The quick-apply trick (why this needs no slot)
This build runs **Queensfoil Censer** — the same artifact as **five** of your presets: Speaker's Sight, Last Well, Dawn Chorus, Consul's Artillery, Starfire. In-game:
1. Apply any of those presets (**Speaker's Sight is the most thematic host** — both are support; **Last Well** on raid nights).
2. **DIM-apply Gravekeeper** on top (one click — gear, subclass, mods).

What carries from those hosts: Heart of the Flame, Torch/Kindling, Revitalizing Blast ✓ — the Solar support engine. You lose **Perpetual Destruction**, which Cenotaph's own intrinsic reload mostly covers anyway. *(Alt host: the **Rain of Fire / Tablet of Ruin** preset for trace-DPS days — its Perpetual Destruction + Gold from Lead carry, and you can set **Harsh Refraction** for real beam damage. Note: Harsh Refraction needs a debuff matching the trace's element — **Acasia's Incandescent scorch self-procs it**; Divinity (Arc) barely benefits on a Solar subclass.)*

## Exotic — Cenotaph Mask
> Your trace rifle slowly reloads from reserves. Trace damage on bosses/champions **marks** them; when a teammate defeats the mark, **they get Heavy ammo and you get Special.**
The strongest invisible support in raids: your two DPS friends simply… always have heavy. Pairs absurdly with your **Last Well** friendcomp (Apex spam funded) and the GG Hunter (his follow-up heavy never dries).

## Weapons
- **Acasia's Dejection** (RoN Solar trace — you own it AND the Adept) — **the default beam**: Incandescent spreads scorch off every mark, chaining with Torch/Kindling artifact perks and feeding Solar Surge + Elemental Siphon. Craftable/enhanceable, and it keeps your exotic weapon slot free.

## Artifact — Queensfoil Censer *(re-evaluated)*

This is a Solar Dawnblade SUPPORT build whose value is radiant/restoration uptime, Well of Radiance, and keeping a Solar trace beam (Acasia's Dejection) glued to the marked target — NOT beam DPS. Queensfoil is the only artifact carrying the full Solar support suite that this loop actually triggers: Heart of the Flame buffs your Well-of-Radiance cast (allies radiant + damage scaling per ally — the core of a support Well), Torch + Kindling Trigger + Rays of Precision turn radiant-uptime into scorch/ignition off the trace, and Fever and Chill/Wished into Being feed radiant and orb generation while you sit in scope. The alternative (Tablet of Ruin) only wins if you want raw trace DPS via Harsh Refraction — but this build explicitly is the ammo/healer, not the DPS, so the debuff-trace beam-damage perk is wasted. Every other artifact (NPA, Encrypted Data Disk, Hunter's Journal, Implement of Curiosity, Slayer Baron's) has ZERO Solar-support/trace synergy. Note: the build doc claims Queensfoil's Perpetual Destruction is column-1 Tier 1 — in this pool it is actually Tier 2 (rocket/GL ammo refund, no trace effect), so I do not lean on it as a trace-sustainer; Cenotaph's intrinsic reload-from-reserves already keeps the beam fed.

## Gameplay Loop
1. **Trace the priority target** → Cenotaph marks it. That's the job.
2. Teammates kill the mark → **they get Heavy, you get Special** → re-mark the next big thing.
3. Between marks: Healing Grenades on the team → Benevolence refunds everything → Healing Rift on cooldown.
4. Damage phase: **Well down**, keep the beam on the boss (Div cage or Prometheus + Harsh Refraction), the team dumps the heavy *you* funded.
