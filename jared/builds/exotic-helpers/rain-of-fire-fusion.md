## The Trinity (ammo economy matters)
- **Vex Mythoclast** (VoG exotic, energy slot) — **PRIMARY ammo**: the infinite fusion workhorse. Solar (feeds Surges + Elemental Siphon), ramps to linear mode on kills.
- **Deliverance** (Vow fusion, kinetic slot) — your **only special-ammo gun**: Chill Clip freeze for champions/stagger. Spend it on problems, not trash.
- **Cataclysmic** (Vow LINEAR fusion, heavy) — Bait and Switch boss DPS into Particle Reconstruction stacks.

## Exotic — Rain of Fire (*Soaring Fusilier*)
> Airborne time + **Icarus Dash reloads your weapons**; **Fusion and Linear Fusion final blows make you Radiant**.
Every dash = a free reload for all three guns; every fusion kill = a damage buff. The build never touches a reload animation: dash-reload + Particle Reconstruction + Perpetual Destruction all refill from reserves.

## Abilities
- **Super: Song of Flame** — abilities supercharged, weapons Radiant. *(Daybreak = the full aerial fantasy alt.)*
- **Class: Phoenix Dive** — cure on landing, zero rift management, and **Powerful Attraction vacuums orbs on every dive**.
- **Grenade: Fusion Grenade** — on-theme filler; Heat Rises fuel.
- **Melee: Incinerator Snap** — ranged scorch between bursts.

### Aspects
1. **Icarus Dash** *(mandatory)* — the reload button. Two charges, refreshed by airborne kills.
2. **Heat Rises** — consume grenade → extended airtime + **airborne effectiveness** (fusions actually land while floating) + Cure on activation + melee energy on airborne kills.

### Fragments (4 slots)
- **Ember of Torches** — powered melee → Radiant backup when fusions are dry.
- **Ember of Empyrean** — Solar kills extend Radiant/Restoration (Vex + Cataclysmic are Solar — Radiant never drops).
- **Ember of Searing** — scorched-target kills → melee energy + Firesprites.
- **Ember of Solace** — longer Radiant/Restoration windows.

## Stat Priority (Armor 3.0)
**Weapons 150→200 > Class ~100 > Super 100 > Health.**
Weapons is the whole build: handling for constant swaps, boss damage over 100, and **double ammo drops at 200** — which directly feeds Cataclysmic, your scarce resource. Class for dive cadence; Super after Weapons is fed.

**Why:** Reload mods are dead weight here — dash-reload, Particle Reconstruction, and Perpetual Destruction already refill from reserves — so arms go to **Dexterity** (you swap guns constantly; swap speed IS this build's handling stat, and Harmonic covers both Solar guns). With one special gun and Gold from Lead in the artifact, special flows on its own — **Heavy Finder feeds Cataclysmic, the actual scarce ammo**. Harmonic Siphon turns Vex's endless primary kills into orbs → **Recuperation** heals on every pickup (patching the build's real weakness) → **Powerful Attraction** grabs them automatically on every dive → **Time Dilation** stretches the Armor Charge so the Solar Surges stay lit between orbs.


## Artifact — Tablet of Ruin *(re-evaluated)*

Unambiguously correct for a Fusion/LFR-centric build, and not by element-matching — it is the ONLY artifact in the pool that carries Particle Reconstruction, the single keystone perk that makes sustained Fusion/Linear Fusion fire stack a self-applied damage debuff against a target while partially reloading the mag from reserves. Every gun in the trinity (Vex Mythoclast, Deliverance, Cataclysmic) procs it, and it is a weapon-specific debuff bucket that stacks on top of Weaken/Tractor/Div, which is what turns this 'meme' into real boss math. Tablet also uniquely holds Gold from Lead (Special-pickup -> Heavy chance, the only thing that keeps scarce Cataclysmic heavy fed) and Limit Break (Solar Super damage while you carry a matching elemental buff — you are permanently Radiant via Rain of Fire). No other artifact has a single fusion-relevant perk; the rest are Arc/Void/Strand/Stasis/Sword/GL packages that touch nothing this build does. The honest caveat: outside ~4 live perks Tablet is thin for this exact loadout, so the tier gate forces a couple of dead 'tax' picks (see notes).

## Gameplay Loop
1. **Jump → Icarus Dash** — all three fusions reload (Soaring Fusilier).
2. **Vex** the room (primary ammo — spray freely) → fusion kills = **Radiant**, Empyrean keeps it rolling.
3. **Deliverance** the dangerous thing — Chill Clip freeze on champions/majors.
4. Airtime low? **Heat Rises** consume → float on, airborne effectiveness up.
5. **Phoenix Dive** → Cure + orb vacuum (Recuperation heal, Armor Charge for Surges) → jump, dash, repeat.

## Boss DPS Rotation (the sleeper)
1. **Pre-stack Particle Reconstruction ×5 with Vex** — it's primary ammo, so stacking the ~28% debuff before the phase costs nothing.
2. Damage phase: **Cataclysmic** Bait-and-Switch bursts into the debuff (dash between mags — instant reloads keep B&S cycling).
3. A teammate's Tractor/Weaken stacks ON TOP of Particle Reconstruction (different buckets) — with Radiant + Surge ×2 this is genuine raid-DPS math, not a meme.
4. Adds spawn? Vex cleanup re-ups Radiant and orbs without touching special/heavy reserves.
