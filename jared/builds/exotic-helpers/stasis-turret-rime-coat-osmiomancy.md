## Exotic — Rime-Coat Raiment (chest) — primary recommendation; Osmiomancy Gloves is the alternative for a pure Coldsnap-spam variant.
Rime-Coat Raiment "Bleak Domain": your Bleak Watcher turret floats higher, periodically spawns Stasis crystals around itself, and projects a ~5m Stasis storm that slows and freezes enemies in the area. Standing in the storm grants you a Stasis "buddy" that fires slowing projectiles at nearby foes (lost ~2s after you leave the storm). The auto-generated crystals are free shatter fuel for Whisper of Shards/Rending → near-permanent grenade energy and Frost Armor. (Osmiomancy Gloves alt: +1 Coldsnap charge, more aggressive tracking, and a direct Coldsnap hit drastically cuts grenade cooldown — best if you prefer manual turret placement over the Rime-Coat storm.)

## Abilities
- **Super:** Winter's Wrath (use sparingly — primarily a freeze/shatter panic button; spec for ability uptime, not Super-centric). Cast for crowd control or to instantly shatter a frozen pack and refill Stasis shards.
- **Class Ability:** Healing Rift — your sustain anchor while you hold position in the Rime-Coat storm. Note: Frostpulse is an *Aspect* (not a Rift type), and this build's two Aspect slots are spent on Bleak Watcher + Glacial Harvest, so your Rift does **not** freeze or grant Frost Armor. Frost Armor instead comes from picking up Stasis shards (9.7.0 system change: shard pickup grants Frost Armor, and taking damage with Frost Armor up returns grenade energy to Warlocks). *Alternate:* swap Glacial Harvest for the Frostpulse Aspect (reworked in 9.7.0: Rift cast freezes nearby enemies and grants 5 stacks of Frost Armor if it freezes, and Frostpulse now has 3 fragment slots) if you want Frost Armor on demand from every Rift.
- **Grenade:** Coldsnap Grenade (the Bleak Watcher fuel of choice — tracking freeze, refunds with Osmiomancy; Rime-Coat turns the turret itself into a freeze storm). Shatter Grenade is a viable swap if you want intrinsic Unstoppable stun from the grenade, but Coldsnap is the optimal turret-loop pick.
- **Melee:** Penumbral Blast — freezes a target on hit to seed shatter chains (and Iceflare Bolts, only if you run that aspect swap).

### Aspects
- Bleak Watcher — hold grenade to spawn a Stasis turret that fires slowing projectiles; the core of the build
- Glacial Harvest — freezing targets creates Stasis shards; in 9.7.0 picking up shatter shards grants Frost Armor (a system-wide change Glacial Harvest feeds), and Frost Armor now enables Stasis/Kinetic weapon shatter and returns grenade energy on damage taken

### Fragments
*Pick four — Bleak Watcher and Glacial Harvest grant two fragment slots each (the Frostpulse-aspect alternate has three slots, opening a fifth).*
- Whisper of Shards — shattering a Stasis crystal massively boosts grenade recharge (stacks with crystal count); the engine that keeps Bleak Watcher and Coldsnap online
- Whisper of Rending — Kinetic/Stasis weapons deal greatly increased shatter damage to Stasis crystals and frozen targets (lets your turret crystals and frozen packs shatter on body shots)
- Whisper of Chains — while near frozen targets or a Stasis crystal, take reduced damage (survivability for GM/Master)
- Whisper of Fissures — increased shatter damage and radius from shattering frozen targets/crystals
- *Flex swap-ins:* Whisper of Torment — taking damage grants grenade energy (over Chains in lower content); Whisper of Conduction — Stasis shards track to you (over Fissures if shard pickup feels inconsistent)

## Weapons
- Verglas Curve (Exotic Stasis bow) — creates Stasis crystals on hits/kills, giving you off-turret crystal fuel for Whisper of Shards/Rending shatter loop
- *Alternative exotic* (only one exotic weapon can be equipped — pick this **or** Verglas Curve): Wicked Implement (Exotic Stasis scout, Heavy Burst → intrinsic Unstoppable in Anti-Champion 2.0) — applies Slow/Frozen (Creeping Attrition) and stuns Unstoppables without an artifact mod
- Cold Comfort / Deliverance or any craftable Stasis Rocket / Wave-frame Stasis weapon for shatter add-clear
- Legendary Stasis Glaive or Stasis SMG (Adaptive/Aggressive frames) for Whisper of Rending body-shot crystal shattering
- A Heavy GL or Rocket (Queensfoil Censer also buffs Rockets/Heavy & Special GLs) for boss DPS while the turret crowd-controls

## Stat Priority
Class > Grenade > Health > Weapons > Melee > Super. Push Class toward 100+ first (faster Rift, better energy gain from all sources, and overshield-on-cast past 100 stacking with Frost Armor for huge survivability). Grenade to ~70 (with Whisper of Shards crystal-shatter refunds, you rarely need more) to keep Bleak Watcher/Coldsnap online. Health to ~100 for Orb HP + flinch resist. Weapons for ammo economy; Melee ~70 floor; Super lowest since this is an ability/turret build, not a Super build.

## Artifact — Queensfoil Censer *(re-evaluated)*

Not an element pick — it's the only artifact whose perk columns simultaneously feed BOTH halves of this build's loop. Column 2/3 carry the entire Stasis-crystal/shatter/Frost-Armor engine (Pillar of Ice, Creeping Chill, Hail The Storm, Frigid Glare, plus Fever and Chill for Stasis-precision Frost Armor), which is the exact economy the Bleak Watcher + Rime-Coat storm runs on: freeze -> shatter -> crystals/shards -> grenade energy (Whisper of Shards) + Frost Armor (Glacial Harvest/shard pickup). The same artifact's Rocket/GL column (Blast Radius, Argent Ordnance, Anti-Champion Nosecone, Perpetual Destruction) buffs the separate Heavy this build leans on for boss DPS, since the turret does little single-target. No other artifact touches Stasis at all — Tablet of Ruin is Arc/Void/Strand, NPA is Strand/Void, etc. Queensfoil is the unique fit.

## Gameplay Loop
1) Cast Healing Rift on contact for sustain, and stack Frost Armor (DR + grenade energy on damage taken) by grabbing the Stasis shards your freezes and shatters drop. (Frostpulse-aspect alternate only: the Rift itself also freezes nearby enemies and grants 5 stacks of Frost Armor.) 2) Hold grenade to drop a Bleak Watcher turret — with Rime-Coat it floats up, projects a freeze storm, spawns Stasis crystals around itself, and gives you a slowing Stasis buddy while you stand in the storm. 3) Enemies get slowed/frozen by the turret + storm; you shatter them with Stasis/Kinetic weapons (Whisper of Rending makes body shots shatter). 4) Shattering crystals/frozen targets → Whisper of Shards floods grenade energy back AND drops Stasis shards → pick up shards for more Frost Armor (Glacial Harvest) and grenade energy (Warlock Frost Armor economy). 5) That refilled grenade immediately becomes the next Bleak Watcher/Coldsnap — the loop self-sustains. 6) Iceflare Bolts (if swapped in) and Penumbral Blast extend freezes; use Winter's Wrath only to reset a tough pack. Against bosses, weaken via your artifact perk and unload a Queensfoil-buffed Rocket/Heavy GL while the turret controls adds.

## Strengths
Near-permanent crowd control — the Rime-Coat turret freeze-storm locks down entire rooms hands-free. Excellent survivability stacking Frost Armor (35% DR at 5 stacks in 9.7.0), Whisper of Chains DR, and 100+ Class overshield. Self-sustaining grenade economy via Whisper of Shards crystal shatters means the turret is almost always available. Scales beautifully into GMs and Onslaught where add density feeds the shatter/shard loop. Anti-Champion 2.0 means Stasis tools (Wicked Implement Unstoppable, or Shatter Grenade Unstoppable — each only if you take that swap) can handle champs intrinsically with no artifact mods.

## Weaknesses
Lower single-target boss DPS than dedicated Super/exotic-weapon builds — you lean on a separate Heavy for damage phases. The loop has a ramp-up; it's weaker before crystals/Frost Armor are established. The active-cooldown bonus cap nerf (190%→125%) in 9.7.0 means you can't infinitely chain grenades from stats alone — you're dependent on crystal shatters, so low-density rooms slow the loop. Standing in the Rime-Coat storm to keep the Stasis buddy can pin you in a bad spot. Freeze does little against many raid/dungeon bosses (CC-immune), reducing it to a DR-and-shatter-for-adds role there.
