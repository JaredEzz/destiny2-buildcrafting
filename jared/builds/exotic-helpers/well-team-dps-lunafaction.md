## What changed in 9.7.0 (the build is engineered around this)
- **Well's 25% empowering damage buff is UNCHANGED** — still a premier team-DPS multiplier.
- **Survivability nerfed hard:** HPS roughly halved (~100→50), but **heal-on-cast greatly increased (~40→300 HP)**; **damage resistance cut to ~20% vs non-boss / ~10% vs bosses.** Well is now offense-first, panic-button second.
- **New: Radiant-on-exit (~8s)** — leaving the Well grants you + nearby allies Radiant, so the team keeps an empowering buff while repositioning.
- **Game-wide: Super energy from boss damage cut 60%.** No more "double Well" in one phase — you build Super from **adds, orbs, and ability loops**, which is why the mod/fragment package below leans hard into Super regen.

## Abilities
- **Rift: Empowering Rift** (mandatory — Lunafaction's range bonus + pre-Well team buff key off it).
- **Grenade: Healing Grenade** (support default; feeds Cure/Restoration) → **Fusion Grenade** for DPS chip.
- **Melee: Incinerator Snap** — ranged scorch, builds melee/super off adds without leaving the Well.

### Aspects
1. **Touch of Flame** — supercharges Healing/Fusion grenade (instant Cure + extended Restoration).
2. **Icarus Dash** — mobility/survivability to reposition now that Well's DR is gutted (keeps the Radiant-on-exit window). *(Hellion is the add-clear alternative.)*

### Fragments
- **Ember of Benevolence** *(keystone)* — applying Cure/Restoration/Radiant to allies massively boosts grenade/melee/class regen → faster Rifts → faster Super.
- **Ember of Torches** — powered melee makes you + allies Radiant (pre-Well team buff).
- **Ember of Empyrean** — Solar kills extend Restoration/Radiant, keeping Benevolence rolling.
- **Ember of Solace** — extends Radiant/Restoration durations.
- **Ember of Mercy** — Restoration to you + nearby allies when you revive someone; Firesprite pickup also grants Restoration (offsets the Well DR nerf).

> Priority (**4 fragment slots** — Touch of Flame 2 + Icarus Dash 2): **Benevolence + Torches + Empyrean**, then Solace **or** Mercy in the last slot.

## Exotic — primary vs alternative
### Lunafaction Boots (PRIMARY — team-DPS enabler)
**Alchemical Etchings:** Rifts also increase **weapon reload speed** (fireteam-wide in the Rift/Well), and Empowering Rift extends **weapon range**. Both directly raise sustained team DPS in reload-limited phases (rockets, LMGs, swords, linears).
> Current version is a strong reload-speed *multiplier*, not the old free instant auto-reload.

### Mantle of Battle Harmony (ALTERNATIVE — personal damage)
**Absorption Cells:** bonus weapon damage while Super is full; matching-element kills grant Super energy. **9.7.0 buff:** now triggers on **sustained damage** (not just final blows) + post-Super all-element surges — so it ramps *during* a boss phase.

**When to pick:** Lunafaction = organized fireteams / reload-limited DPS / raids+dungeons. Mantle = solo-ish, you're the primary DPS, or the team already has another Well.

## Weapons (team-DPS template)
*You can equip only **one exotic weapon** — pick a loadout:*
- **Solar Wave-Frame GL (Demolitionist + Incandescent)** — energy-slot legendary for ad-clear between damage phases: Incandescent scorch + Demolitionist grenade refunds. Not your DPS weapon (that is the heavy below), but a strong Solar utility pick.
- **Loadout A — you're the debuffer: Tractor Cannon (exotic, POWER slot)** = **30% Weaken** (strongest debuff after Divinity's 15% nerf) — one shot multiplies the whole team's damage. Tractor occupies your heavy AND exotic slot, so run legendary primaries; your "DPS" is the team buff.
- **Loadout B — a teammate runs Tractor/Div:** **Apex Predator** (Bait-and-Switch/Bipod/Reconstruction) heavy is the gold standard; **Gjallarhorn** for fireteam Wolfpack rounds; LFRs/swords for stationary bosses. If your heavy is legendary (Apex/LFR/sword), the freed exotic slot can take **Bad Juju** — String of Curses grants Super energy on kills; refills Well off adds now that boss damage gives 60% less — or **Witherhoard** for chip damage.

## Stat Priority (Armor 3.0)
**Super > Class > Weapons > Health > Grenade/Melee.**
- **Super** — Well up fast (can't lean on boss damage anymore).
- **Class** — faster Empowering Rifts → more pre-Well buff + orbs/regen.
- **Weapons** — reload/handling + ammo economy for DPS.
- **Health** — offsets the Well DR nerf.
> Cooldown bonus from stats now caps at 125% (was 190%) — spread, don't dump. All exotics are retroactively Tier 5 (no re-grind for Lunafaction/Mantle).

## Gameplay Loop
**Build Super (between phases):** Empowering Rift on adds (Bomber refunds grenade energy on the cast) → Incinerator Snap + Solar kills proc Benevolence/Empyrean + make orbs (Solar Siphon) → pick up orbs (Recuperation/Innervation) → refill Rift + Super; on Loadout B, Bad Juju kills inject Super directly. **Kill adds aggressively — boss damage gives 60% less Super.**

**DPS phase:**
1. **Tractor Cannon** the boss → 30% Weaken (your shot on Loadout A; a teammate's on Loadout B).
2. **Drop Well** on the stack point → 25% buff + big heal-on-cast.
3. Team reloads fast + extended range inside **Lunafaction** → dump Apex/LFR/sword.
4. Reposition if needed → **~8s Radiant-on-exit** keeps the buff in transit.
5. Re-stack, reload, repeat.

## ⚠ Stacking rule (maximize damage)
- **Empowering buffs DON'T stack** — only the highest applies. Well's 25% and Radiant share the slot → stacking Radiant on Well adds **nothing**. Team just needs *one* empowering buff (Well inside, Radiant-on-exit in transit).
- **One debuff per enemy** — use the strongest → **Tractor's 30% Weaken** (beats Divinity 15%).
- **What DOES stack multiplicatively:** Empower (Well 25%) × Debuff (Tractor 30%) × **Surge mods** (Solar Surge legs). That product is the build's team-DPS ceiling.

## Strengths / Weaknesses
**Strengths:** Highest team-DPS multiplier package (Well + Lunafaction + Tractor + Surges, all stacking correctly); Lunafaction uniquely raises the *whole fireteam's* sustained DPS; easy Super loop off adds.
**Weaknesses:** Well's survivability gutted (position carefully); no double-Well per phase (60% Super nerf); Lunafaction is dead weight outside DPS; low *personal* damage (that's what Mantle is for).
**Difficulty:** Low–Moderate — simple to play, but timing the Well drop + building Super off adds matters more than pre-9.7.0.
