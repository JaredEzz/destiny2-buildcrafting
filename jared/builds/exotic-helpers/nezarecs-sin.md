## Exotic — Nezarec's Sin (Helmet)
Abyssal Extractors: Void-damage kills boost ability AND Super recharge rate. In Update 9.7.0 each Void kill grants ~2.5s of Abyssal Extractors (up to ~300% ability recharge / ~200% Super recharge), stackable to ~20s. Reworked Soul Siphon synergy: Soul Siphon damage Suppresses targets, and defeating Suppressed targets — or killing with Soul Siphon itself — extends Abyssal Extractors by ~1.5s, letting the loop self-sustain off the new aspect.

## Abilities
- **Super:** Nova Bomb: Cataclysm (tracking detonation for boss/ad clear; swap to Nova Bomb: Vortex for stationary DPS and weaken-pool uptime in raids/dungeons)
- **Class Ability:** Healing Rift (pairs with Child of the Old Gods to spawn the Void Soul; Class stat over 100 also grants an overshield on every cast)
- **Grenade:** Vortex Grenade (with Echo of Remnants for long-lasting weaken pools; Echo of Undermining makes it Weaken on contact)
- **Melee:** Pocket Singularity (two charges; the powered melee that arms the new Soul Siphon aspect)

### Aspects
- Soul Siphon (NEW Monument of Triumph Voidwalker aspect — after a powered melee, press melee again to drain up to ~4 enemies for damage, Void Overshield, and class-ability energy; drained targets are Suppressed)
- Child of the Old Gods (cast Rift to spawn a Void Soul that seeks enemies, Weakens and Suppresses them, and returns ability energy; reworked to move 100% faster and now grants extra Fragment slots — together with Soul Siphon this build runs five Fragments)

### Fragments
- Echo of Undermining (Void grenades Weaken targets)
- Echo of Remnants (lingering grenade/Vortex effects last longer — extends weaken-pool and Devour-feeding uptime)
- Echo of Starvation (picking up an Orb of Power or a Void Breach grants Devour — this is the build's Devour source, since Feed the Void doesn't fit in the two aspect slots; Void Siphon Orbs and Unto the Breach Breaches keep it permanently fed)
- Echo of Expulsion (Void ability final blows make targets explode — extra add-clear that re-triggers Nezarec's Sin and Devour)
- Echo of Instability (grenade damage grants Volatile Rounds — chains Void damage so weapon kills count toward Abyssal Extractors; fits in the extra slots Child of the Old Gods provides)

## Weapons
- Graviton Lance (Exotic Pulse — Void final blows spawn tracking projectiles that explode for chain Void kills, supercharging Abyssal Extractors and Devour; the classic Nezarec's Sin partner)
- Le Monarque or Wish-Keeper (alternative Exotic Void Bows if you'd rather not run Graviton Lance — only one Exotic weapon can be equipped; poison/weaken pressure for tougher targets)
- Funnelweb / any Void Lightweight or Adaptive Auto (intrinsic Overload via the new frame-based anti-champion; great Volatile Rounds delivery)
- A Void Heavy GL or Machine Gun (e.g. Commemoration) for boss DPS — Void element keeps the Abyssal Extractors loop fed during damage phases
- Any Void Special (Riptide-style Fusion or a Void Sniper) — Void element ensures kills extend Abyssal Extractors

## Stat Priority
Class 100+ (push past 100 for the overshield-on-Rift second effect and faster energy gain) > Melee ~70-100 (Soul Siphon drain damage and energy return scale with the Melee stat; aim 100 if you want it as a damage tool) > Health ~70-100 (survivability floor in GMs) > Grenade ~70 (baseline so Vortex/weaken behaves normally) > Super > Weapons. The build is ability-regen-driven, so the priority is Class and Melee, then a Health/Grenade floor.

## Artifact — NPA Repulsion Regulator *(re-evaluated)*

This build's entire engine is Void debuffs (Vortex Weaken, Soul Siphon Suppress, Volatile via Echo of Instability) feeding Devour (Echo of Starvation off Orbs/Breaches), overshields, and Abyssal Extractors ability/Super regen. NPA Repulsion Regulator is the only artifact whose perk economy converts those debuff-kills into the resources the build runs on: Unto the Breach mints Void Breaches off every Weakened/Suppressed/Volatile kill, Protective Breach turns each Breach into an overshield (stacking with Soul Siphon's), Supernova turns a Breach into a free area-Weaken, and Void Weapon Channeling rewards the build's permanently-charged ability state with up to +25% Void weapon damage. It is a closed loop: debuffs -> Breaches/Orbs -> overshield + Devour + weapon damage -> more Void kills -> more Abyssal Extractors stacks. No other artifact in the pool (e.g. Encrypted Data Disk's sword/Arc-leaning Void perks like Singularity Blade/Void Infestation) gives this Breach economy that the build is literally built around — the choice is mechanical, not element-matching. The doc already lands on NPA and that is correct.

## Gameplay Loop
1) Cast Healing Rift on a group — Child of the Old Gods spawns a Void Soul that flies out to Weaken/Suppress enemies and feed ability energy (and, at Class 100+, you gain an overshield). 2) Throw the Vortex Grenade into the group: Echo of Undermining Weakens them, Echo of Instability grants Volatile Rounds, and Echo of Remnants keeps the pool alive. 3) Land a Pocket Singularity powered melee to arm Soul Siphon, then press melee again to drain ~4 enemies — dealing damage, Suppressing them, and returning class-ability energy + Void Overshield. 4) Clean up with Volatile-Rounds Void weapons (Graviton Lance). Picking up the Orbs and Void Breaches your kills generate grants Devour via Echo of Starvation (then every kill while Devoured fully heals you and refunds grenade energy), and every Void kill stacks Abyssal Extractors (huge ability + Super recharge). Defeating the Suppressed targets Soul Siphon created extends Abyssal Extractors further. 5) Echo of Expulsion explosions and Void Breaches (from Volatile kills) keep the chain rolling — Breaches feed Protective Breach overshields and Supernova weaken pulses. Net result: Rift, grenade, and melee are almost always available, you sit near-permanent Devour + overshield, and your Super returns fast for Nova Bomb on the next pack or boss.

## Strengths
Outstanding ability uptime — Abyssal Extractors plus Soul Siphon's class-energy return and Child of the Old Gods make Rift/grenade/melee near-constant, with very fast Super regen. Excellent survivability stack: permanent Devour healing, Soul Siphon + Protective Breach overshields, and Suppression/Weaken control on demand. Strong, scalable ad-clear (Volatile chains, Expulsion explosions, Vortex weaken pools) and a built-in boss debuff via Weaken for raid/dungeon DPS. Forgiving in GMs because the loop self-heals and crowd-controls. Frame-based anti-champion means you bring Overload/Barrier/Unstoppable for free without artifact mods.

## Weaknesses
Melee-centric: Soul Siphon wants you in mid-range, which is riskier against snipers/heavy ranged in GMs. The loop needs Void kills to ramp — against very tanky single targets (bosses with no adds) Abyssal Extractors stalls and Devour can drop. No top-tier burst Super for boss DPS (Nova Bomb is a one-and-done; this build is sustain/ad-clear, not a damage-phase king like Song of Flame or the Skull double-Nova combo). Relies on staying at high Class/Melee stats to feel its best, and post-9.7.0 ability-cooldown cap nerfs mean you can't fully overcap regen. Reduced effectiveness in low-add-density encounters.
