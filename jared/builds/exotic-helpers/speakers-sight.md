## Exotic — Speaker's Sight (Helmet)
Converts your Healing Grenade into a deployable Healing Turret. The turret fires a tracking Seeker roughly every 3 seconds that grants Cure and Restoration to you and nearby allies, and generates an Orb of Power on a short cooldown. With Touch of Flame the turret heals harder and applies x2 Restoration; this is your perpetual, hands-off team-healing and Orb engine. In Update 9.7.0 the exotic drops at Tier 5 stats with all tuning mods unlocked, so no re-grind for a better roll.

## Abilities
- **Super:** Well of Radiance (primary, for raids/team content) — swap to Song of Flame for solo/boss-DPS phases where a stationary Well is risky
- **Class Ability:** Phoenix Dive (Healing Rift as alternate for a fixed safe-zone in static encounters)
- **Grenade:** Healing Grenade (converted into a Healing Turret/Seeker by Speaker's Sight)
- **Melee:** Incinerator Snap

### Aspects
- Touch of Flame (mandatory — empowers Healing Grenade for x2 Restoration + Cure and stronger turret output)
- Icarus Dash (mobility/repositioning — a quick dodge to break line of sight and get to teammates who need heals; avoid Hellion since the Solar buddy steals kills and starves your Orb/ammo economy)

### Fragments
- Ember of Benevolence (healing/buffing allies grants +400% ability regen for 6s — near-100% uptime is the engine of the build)
- Ember of Torches (powered melee makes you Radiant — always-on Solar staple)
- Ember of Empyrean (Solar weapon/ability kills extend your own Restoration/Radiant — it only affects you, but it keeps the healer alive between turret heals)
- Ember of Mercy (grant Restoration to nearby allies on reviving, and gain Restoration on Firesprite pickup — extra team healing)

## Weapons
- **Solar Wave-Frame GL (Demolitionist + Incandescent)** — your area-denial GL: Incandescent spreads Scorch (feeding ignitions/Firesprites) while Demolitionist refunds grenade energy. Excellent legendary fit for this build's Solar scorch/grenade economy.
- Any Incandescent legendary primary (scorch stacking feeds Ignitions and team-wide adds clear) — e.g. a Solar Pulse or Auto
- A Solar Special with Incandescent or a healing-synergy perk
- Boss-DPS Heavy with intrinsic anti-champion frame under Anti-Champion 2.0 (Rocket-Assisted, Heavy Burst, or Aggressive frame for Unstoppable; Xenophage as an exotic option)
- Exotic flex (only if NOT running Xenophage — one exotic weapon at a time): support/utility exotic such as a wave-frame GL or any Solar exotic that benefits from your near-permanent Radiant

## Stat Priority
Class → Grenade → Super → Health → Weapons → Melee. Push Class past 100 (overshield on every Phoenix Dive/Rift cast + best energy gain from all sources) and Grenade to ~100 to spam the turret; Super high to keep Well/Song of Flame online for the team; Health 100+ for the Orb-HP and survivability layer in GM/Master content.

## Artifact — Queensfoil Censer *(re-evaluated)*

Correct pick, but for a different reason than the build doc states. Reading the authoritative pool, this is the ONLY artifact whose perks key off Radiant — which is exactly what this build manufactures with near-permanent uptime (Ember of Torches melee + Fever and Chill precision + Rays of Precision). On every other artifact that Radiant does nothing. Here it converts into real output: Kindling Trigger turns Radiant into a free Solar-weapon Scorch applicator (the add-clear engine), Rays of Precision turns Radiant Solar precision kills into Ignitions, Fever and Chill makes Radiant self-sustaining off any Solar primary, and Heart of the Flame extends the build's healer identity to the fireteam by granting team-wide Radiant on Well/Song of Flame cast. No element-matching reasoning needed — it's the only artifact whose mechanics are gated on the buff this build keeps at 100% uptime.

## Gameplay Loop
1) Throw your Healing Grenade — Speaker's Sight deploys a Healing Turret that auto-fires Seekers, healing the team (x2 Restoration via Touch of Flame), generating Orbs of Power. 2) Granting those heals/buffs to allies procs Ember of Benevolence (+400% ability regen), which refunds your grenade almost instantly — re-deploy the turret to keep 100% uptime. 3) Use a powered melee (Ember of Torches) and Solar precision kills (Rays of Precision) to stay permanently Radiant; Incandescent weapons scorch and Ignite packs for add clear. 4) Phoenix Dive (or Healing Rift) on cooldown for the Class-stat overshield and extra Cure pulses; Icarus Dash to reposition safely. 5) In DPS phases drop Well of Radiance (team-wide Radiant + sustained healing in the Well) — or Song of Flame for mobile boss fights where a stationary Well is unsafe. 6) Repeat: turret down, heal, refund, re-throw — you are a perpetual healing/Orb/Radiant battery for the fireteam.

## Strengths
Best-in-game sustained team healing and survivability for GMs, raids, and dungeons; near-100% Restoration/Radiant uptime; constant Orb generation feeds the whole fireteam's mods; massive DPS uplift in burn phases via Well's team-wide Radiant; Tier 5 exotic + Armor 3.0 means top stats with no re-grind; very low input cost for the value.

## Weaknesses
Low personal/single-target damage — leans on the fireteam for DPS; Healing Turret is stationary, so highly mobile or one-shot mechanics can outrun its coverage; Well of Radiance is a static safe-zone that some modern GM/raid mechanics punish (mitigate by swapping to Song of Flame); under the 9.7.0 ability-economy nerfs (active cooldown cap cut to 125%) you must keep Ember of Benevolence active or the turret-refund loop slows; less self-sufficient solo than a damage build.
