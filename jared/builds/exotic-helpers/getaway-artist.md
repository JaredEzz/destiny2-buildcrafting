## Exotic — Getaway Artist (gauntlets)
Consume your Arc grenade to become Amplified and summon a sentient Arc Soul that fires faster and tracks targets. The Arc Soul lasts longer and can apply Jolt (via Facet of Dominance). On Prismatic with Bleak Watcher equipped, a single held grenade input consumes your Storm Grenade and spawns BOTH the Getaway Arc Soul and a Stasis turret at once — two autonomous turrets plus Amplified damage resistance from one cast. In 9.7.0 all Armor 3.0 exotics drop at Tier 5 stats and unlock all tuning mods, so no roll re-grind is needed.

## Abilities
- **Super:** Song of Flame (default — team Radiant/ability-regen + boss DPS window). Swap to Stormtrance or Needlestorm if you prefer a damage super; Song of Flame is the endgame pick for support + ability uptime.
- **Class Ability:** Healing Rift (default for survivability in GM/Master). Use Phoenix Dive only if running a Solar-leaning variant. With Class stat >100 you also get an overshield on every Rift cast.
- **Grenade:** Storm Grenade (the only Arc grenade available on Prismatic Warlock, and Getaway Artist requires an Arc grenade to consume for the Arc Soul + Amplified. With Bleak Watcher slotted, one held grenade input consumes it and spawns both the Arc Soul and a Stasis turret simultaneously — you do not alternate casts.)
- **Melee:** Arcane Needle (Strand melee — three charges that track and Sever targets; its final blows proc Devour) OR Chain Lightning (Arc) if going pure-Arc. Arcane Needle is the Prismatic default.

### Aspects
- Bleak Watcher (hold grenade = Stasis turret; pairs with Getaway Artist's consumed-grenade Arc Soul for twin turrets)
- Feed the Void (ability kills grant enhanced Devour for constant healing + grenade energy)

### Fragments
(4 slots: Bleak Watcher and Feed the Void grant 2 fragment slots each)
- Facet of Courage (your Light abilities deal increased damage to targets afflicted by Darkness debuffs — the Arc Soul hits harder on targets Bleak Watcher has slowed/frozen)
- Facet of Dominance (your Void grenades Weaken and your Arc Souls Jolt targets — NOTE 9.7.0 added a ~4s internal cooldown to Arc Soul jolt, so the loop is capped but still strong)
- Facet of Protection (damage resistance near enemies — GM survivability)
- Facet of Mending (grenade final blows grant Cure — extra healing layered on top of Devour)

## Weapons
- Indebted Kindness (Arc Special Sidearm, Voltshot/Demolitionist — top-tier add-clear + grenade refund)
- Centrifuse or any Arc exotic primary for ad-clear and Amplified synergy
- An Arc SMG/AR with Voltshot (e.g. IKELOS SMG) to refresh Amplified and apply Jolt
- Heavy: a Rocket-Assisted or Aggressive frame (intrinsic Unstoppable in 9.7.0) such as Apex Predator / a Wave-frame GL like Forbearance for ad-clear
- For boss DPS: any high-DPS Heavy whose frame intrinsically stuns the champion type present (anti-champion is now frame-intrinsic — no artifact mods)

## Stat Priority
Class (Recovery) to 100+ for fast Rift + overshield-on-cast, then Grenade ~70+ to keep the Getaway/Bleak Watcher turret loop fed, then Health to 100 for orb-HP and flinch resist. Suggested order: Class > Grenade > Health > Melee > Super > Weapons. Use Demolitionist/Reaver archetype armor (Grenade/Class focus).

## Artifact — Tablet of Ruin *(re-evaluated)*

It is the only one of the seven artifacts that holds the COMPLETE Bolt Charge / Amplified package this build's engine is built on: Dielectric (Arc-debuffed kills -> Bolt Charge), Flashover (max Bolt Charge -> Amplified + harder bolts), and Defibrillating Blast (champ stun -> max Bolt Charge) all live here together. The build's whole loop is Arc Soul jolt -> jolted kills -> Bolt Charge -> Amplified, and Tablet is the artifact that converts that jolt into Bolt Charge, healing, Orbs, and stacked Amplified DR. Encrypted Data Disk has Dielectric (T2) but no Flashover, so Bolt Charge dead-ends; Hunter's Journal/NPA/Implement/Slayer/Queensfoil are Sword/Void/Stasis/Solar-themed and never close the Bolt Charge -> Amplified circuit. Picked on mechanics, not element-matching.

## Gameplay Loop
1) Cast Healing Rift. 2) Hold the grenade input: Getaway Artist consumes your Storm Grenade to spawn the sentient Arc Soul and make you Amplified (damage resistance). 3) Bleak Watcher triggers on that same held cast, deploying a Stasis turret alongside the Arc Soul — twin autonomous turrets from one input; repeat each time your grenade returns. 4) Get an ability kill to proc enhanced Devour (Feed the Void) for constant healing + grenade energy. 5) Arc Soul jolts targets (Facet of Dominance) → jolted/slowed kills feed Bolt Charge via Tablet of Ruin's Dielectric → at max Bolt Charge, Flashover keeps you Amplified passively. 6) Devour's grenade energy on kills + grenade mods (Innervation, Grenade Kickstart, Bomber) keep grenade energy topped so you can re-consume for a fresh Arc Soul, while Facet of Mending's Cure on grenade final blows layers extra healing. 7) Pick up Orbs for Class/grenade energy. 8) Use Song of Flame on bosses for team Radiant + a DPS window. The loop self-sustains: turrets do passive damage while you focus weapon kills and stay healed via Devour.

## Strengths
Extremely high uptime on passive damage (two turrets) with near-constant Devour healing and Amplified damage resistance, making it one of the most survivable and AFK-friendly endgame builds. Excellent ad-clear and crowd control (Bleak Watcher freezes/slows, Arc Soul jolts). Strong in GM Nightfalls, Onslaught, and as a support/DPS hybrid in raids via Song of Flame. Anti-champion is now frame-intrinsic in 9.7.0, so any correct-frame weapon stuns champs with no artifact dependence.

## Weaknesses
Lower personal burst DPS than dedicated damage builds (turrets are sustained, not burst) — boss DPS leans on Song of Flame and your heavy weapon. The 9.7.0 ~4-second internal cooldown on Arc Soul jolt (Facet of Dominance / Spark of Shock) caps the old infinite-jolt loop, so jolt application is slower than in prior seasons. Active ability-cooldown bonus cap was cut (190%→125%) in 9.7.0, so over-stacking grenade mods has diminishing returns. Relies on Getaway Artist exotic, so you give up an exotic slot. Pure-Arc Stormcaller variant (Tablet of Ruin focus, Chain Lightning + Stormtrance) is an alternative if you want full Arc identity but loses Bleak Watcher's twin-turret/freeze control.
