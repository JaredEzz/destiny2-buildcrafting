# 🕷️ Spider-Man — Grapple-Tangle-Melee Warlock (FOR FUN, tuned for DPS + add-clear)

## Verdict
Quicksilver Storm makes tangles, you grapple to them, the lunge webs the room, and every punch-kill heals you off Karnstein. **It is the Spider-Man fantasy, real and mechanically sound — not built on a myth.**

- **Subclass winner: STRAND BROODWEAVER** (decisively). Prismatic can't keep the grapple-egg engine, Needlestorm, AND a turret in 2 aspect slots — locking Karnstein + Grapple amputates Prismatic's tangle economy for zero gain Strand can't match.
- **FUN: 9/10** — elite swing-and-punch add-clear; only dips when a lone boss leaves no tangles to swing on.
- **POWER: 7/10** — 9/10 add-clear, 8/10 room CC, but boss DPS is fully outsourced to your heavy. You are the room-control specialist, not the damage dealer.
- **Ceiling: Legend out-of-box; Master-capable only after the punch-list** (champion mods on the artifact + a real boss heavy).

> **The one truth that makes it click:** the grapple *lunge* does NOT suspend — it unravels, drops 3 Threadling eggs, and makes a Tangle. You web the pack by **popping/throwing that Tangle** (The Wanderer, 7m suspend). Build muscle memory around *throw-the-web*, not punch-the-web.

### Aspects (2)
1. **Mindspun Invocation** — grapple-melee spawns **3 Threadling eggs** per lunge. The only aspect that touches grapple. *(Honest note: you're using ~1/3 of its value — the Shackle/Weaver's-Trance paths need a grenade slot you've given to Grapple. Still correct; nothing else feeds the grapple.)*
2. **The Wanderer** — Threadling kills spawn Tangles (and cut tangle cooldown 1s/kill in 9.7.0); **throwing/destroying a Tangle suspends everything in 7m.** Your suspend button AND your catalyst-insurance tangle engine.

*Survivability swap:* The Wanderer → **Weavewalk** (airborne DR) per-encounter in brutal Master rooms — but you lose your suspend engine, so it's situational, not default.

### Fragments (4)
1. **Thread of Generation** — grenade energy on weapon damage. Warlock has NO 2nd grapple charge (that aspect is Hunter-only), so this + grapple-to-tangle refunds IS your uptime economy. Non-negotiable.
2. **Thread of Continuity** — extends suspend/unravel/sever duration → packs stay webbed longer.
3. **Thread of Warding** — Woven Mail on orb pickup → the DR that turns the glass cannon into a Master-survivable one.
4. **Thread of Fury** — melee energy on absorbing a Tangle → keeps Arcane Needle (suspend + heal) charged between grapples.

*Runners-up:* **Thread of Mind** (class energy on suspend kills — swap over Warding if comfy on survivability) · **Thread of Evolution** (Threadling damage — swap over Fury if drowning in melee energy).

## Gameplay Loop

### Add-clear a room (the bread & butter — 9/10)
1. **Open with QSS / Strand special** on the pack → kills spawn **Tangles** (catalyst) + Threadlings.
2. **Grapple to a Tangle** (FREE — costs zero grenade) → grapple-melee lunge → **unravels + 3 Threadling eggs + makes a new Tangle**, and any melee kill procs **Karnstein cure+restoration.**
3. **Throw/destroy a Tangle** (The Wanderer) → **7m suspend** webs the whole pack. Continuity keeps them locked.
4. Suspended adds die to **Arcane Needle / QSS / Threadlings** → more tangles → **swing to the next pack and repeat.** You are healing on every punch-kill.

### DPS a boss (the honest weak phase — outsourced to heavy)
1. **No adds = no free tangles** — your swing loop goes quiet. Accept it.
2. **Cast Needlestorm** (suspends minor bosses/champs only — most raid/GM bosses are immune; treat it as sustained damage).
3. **Empty your heavy** — Bait-and-Switch rocket / Strand DPS LMG. **This is the actual damage.**
4. **QSS rocket rounds** to fill, **Healing Rift** to stay up, **finisher** for a panic heal. Repeat. In coordinated content, let a teammate be the DPS — you own the room.

## Tips / Failure Modes
- **Grapple goes down in low-density** (lone champ, between waves): the free-grapple loop is gated on add density. No adds → full grenade cost + full cooldown. Lean on **Grenade Kickstart + Bomber + Thread of Generation** to bridge it.
- **Champions:** Suspend = great vs **Overload**, useless vs **Barrier**, and won't stun **Unstoppable** alone. **Patch the artifact with champion mods for Master, or carry the verbs on QSS/legendaries.** This is the #1 thing standing between you and GM-ready.
- **When NOT to melee in:** never melee-*open* on a tanky major/champ — **Karnstein heals on the KILL, not the swing.** Soften with QSS/special/heavy, then **punch as the EXECUTE** for the heal. **Finisher = guaranteed panic heal** (invuln during animation). **Rift BEFORE the dive, not after you're chunked.**
- **Tangle tension:** a Tangle you *throw* to suspend is one you *didn't* grapple to — in lean moments you're trading mobility for CC from the same resource. Fine in add-dense content; ration it when sparse.
- **No QSS catalyst?** The build still functions on The Wanderer (Threadling→Tangle) + a Hatchling Strand special + grapple-melee tangles. Farm the catalyst; until then The Wanderer is mandatory, not optional.
- **Fallback subclass — Prismatic Warlock:** only if Master is killing you faster than you can heal and you want a hands-off turret. Keep Karnstein + Grapple + Arcane Needle, run **The Wanderer + Bleak Watcher**, super Song of Flame. You trade Needlestorm + Mindspun eggs + half the tangle economy for Transcendence, Devour, and a freeze turret babysitting the room. **More set-and-forget survivability, less swing-through-the-room webbing.**
