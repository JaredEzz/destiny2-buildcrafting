# 🌅 Dawn Chorus + One Thousand Voices — Solar Sustained Boss-DPS

**Concept:** Drop a Well, Tractor the boss, and hold the 1KV beam — continuous Solar damage that scorches into ignitions, with Dawn Chorus cranking every ignition. Radiant × Weaken × amplified-ignitions stacked on top of an already-elite beam.

| Metric | Grade |
|---|---|
| **OVERALL** | **A** |
| Raw DPS (sustained) | **A** (1KV is a current top-tier DPS weapon) |
| Practicality / consistency | **B+** |
| Survivability | **A−** |
| Future-proofing | **A** (no glitch — stable, recently buffed) |

> This is the **reliable, future-proof** counterpart to the spicy Song of Flame melt (A−, glitch-dependent burst). This one is *sustained* DPS and won't get patched out from under you.

---

## Loadout — Solar (Dawnblade)
| Slot | Pick | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Exotic** | **Dawn Chorus** (helmet) | Scorch deals more damage + ignitions hit harder; 1KV's beam scorches (catalyst) → Dawn-Chorus-amplified ignitions on top of the beam. |
| **Super** | **Well of Radiance** | The DPS super — Radiant (+weapon dmg) + a stand-and-beam safe platform. (Dawn Chorus buffs Daybreak, but you're beaming, not flying — Well wins for 1KV.) |
| **Aspects** | **Touch of Flame** + **Heat Rises** | Touch of Flame = bigger ignitions (Dawn Chorus loves them); Heat Rises = ability uptime + survivability. |
| **Fragments** | **Ember of Ashes** (faster scorch stacks → faster ignitions) · **Ember of Searing** (scorch kills → ability energy) · **Ember of Torches** (melee → Radiant) · **Ember of Empyrean** (Solar kills extend Restoration/Radiant) |
| **Grenade / Melee** | Healing or Fusion / Incinerator Snap (scorch) |
| **Class / Movement** | Phoenix Dive (heal, no rift-swap) — or Healing Rift inside the Well / Icarus Dash |

## Weapons (1KV is a **Power/heavy** exotic — it's your ONLY exotic)
| Slot | Weapon | Job |
|---|---|---|
| **Power** | **One Thousand Voices** (+ catalyst) | The DPS beam. 1KV is your exotic → the other two slots must be **Legendary**. |
| **Kinetic** | **Midnight Coup / Ignition Code** (Legendary kinetic) | Ammo + add-clear while you reload/re-Well. |
| **Energy** | **Ammit AR2** (Solar) or a Solar scorch primary | Pre-ramps scorch for Dawn-Chorus-boosted ignitions. |

> ⚠️ **Tractor Cannon does NOT fit** — it's *also* a Power/heavy exotic, so it collides with 1KV (two heavies + two exotics). Solo, you skip the 30% weaken and lean on **Well Radiant + ignitions**; in a fireteam, have a teammate bring the Tractor/Divinity weaken.

## Stats (0–200): **Super > Weapons > Health > Class**
Super = Well uptime (the DPS super); Weapons = 1KV reload/ready; Health = survive the beam window; Class = Dive/Rift uptime.

## Artifact — Hunter's Journal
**Solar Fulmination** (ignition chains + damage — the keystone) · Radiant Orbs · Shieldcrush · Elemental Siphon · + survivability/Void-weaken synergy perks. (Same Solar Fulmination engine as the Eunoia build.)

## DPS Rotation
1. **Tractor Cannon** the boss → 30% Weaken.
2. **Well of Radiance** → Radiant (+25% weapon dmg).
3. **Hold the 1KV beam** on the boss — Solar damage + stacking scorch.
4. Scorch → **ignitions**, amplified by **Dawn Chorus + Solar Fulmination**, all multiplied by Radiant × Weaken.
5. **Refresh Tractor** + reload between heavy bricks; Dive/Rift to stay topped.

## Caveats
- **Heavy-ammo gated** — 1KV eats heavy ammo (fusion); bring Heavy Finder / brick economy or you run dry mid-phase.
- **Stationary beaming** — Well covers survivability, but melee-hostile bosses still threaten you.
- **Sustained, not burst** — shines on long DPS phases; for short burst windows a Bait-and-Switch rocket can match or beat it on total.
- Needs **One Thousand Voices** (Last Wish) + ideally its catalyst.

### ✅ Verdict
**A — farm it.** 1KV is regarded as one of the best DPS weapons in the game right now; Dawn Chorus + Well + Tractor is the cleanest way to maximize it, and unlike the Song-of-Flame melt, none of it is glitch-dependent.
