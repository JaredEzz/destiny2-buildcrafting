## Exotic — Necrotic Grip
Melee hits poison the target; defeating a poisoned enemy spreads the poison to nearby enemies. Paired with Arcane Needle's Unravel and a swarm of Threadlings, kills chain poison + unravel across entire rooms, turning add-clear into a self-sustaining wipe. Alternate exotic: Swarmers (destroying a Tangle spawns a Threadling, and your Threadlings unravel targets) for a purer threadling/unravel build with less melee dependence.

## Abilities
- **Super:** Needlestorm — fires a volley of strand needles that detonate into Threadlings; excellent for add-clear and respectable boss DPS via the threadling barrage. Use it as a panic/reset button and to flood the field with extra Threadlings.
- **Class Ability:** Healing Rift (committed — pairs with Woven Mail for Master/GM survivability; no rift-swapping)
- **Grenade:** Threadling Grenade (core — consumed/charged by Mindspun Invocation to deploy the Threadling Nest turret). Shackle Grenade is the alternate for heavy CC content.
- **Melee:** Arcane Needle (3 charges of Unravel application; with Necrotic Grip each hit also seeds poison that spreads on kills)

### Aspects
- Mindspun Invocation — REWORKED this update: holding the grenade input converts the charged Threadling Grenade into a persistent Threadling Nest that acts like a turret, continuously spawning Threadlings; also enhances grapple-melee (spawns threadling eggs) and Shackle interactions
- Weaver's Call — cast Rift to weave new Threadlings and release any perched Threadlings all at once; the backbone of Threadling generation

### Fragments
- Thread of Evolution — Threadlings deal more damage and travel farther (mandatory; this is the build's primary damage multiplier)
- Thread of Generation — dealing damage generates grenade energy, keeping the Nest/Threadling Grenade online
- Thread of Warding — picking up an Orb of Power grants Woven Mail for damage resistance (key for GM survivability)
- Thread of Fury — damaging targets with a Tangle grants melee energy, feeding more Unravel/poison procs
- Thread of Rebirth — Threadling final blows have a chance to spawn a new Threadling (snowballs the swarm in add-dense content)

## Weapons
- **Lost Signal** (legendary Stasis Area-Denial GL) — bouncing area-denial projectiles for relentless add-clear. Note it's Stasis, so it doesn't feed your Strand/Unravel economy or Elemental Overdrive — take it only when sheer area denial matters more than mono-Strand synergy.
- Service of Luzaku (Exotic Strand MG) — explicitly buffed to interact with the reworked Mindspun Invocation/Threadling Nest this update; top-tier pairing for this build
- Final Warning (Exotic Strand sidearm) — applies Unravel and tracks, strong primary-slot synergy if not running Service of Luzaku
- **The Call** (legendary Strand Rocket-Assisted Sidearm) — roll **Hatchling** (final blows spawn Threadlings) + **Slice** (applies Sever): a primary-slot Threadling factory that stacks on top of the Threadling Nest, Necrotic poison spread, and Thread of Rebirth. Slot it as your primary if you'd rather run it than an exotic — genuine synergy here.
- A Strand Aggressive/High-Impact frame for intrinsic Unstoppable champion coverage (Anti-Champion 2.0)
- A Strand Precision/Adaptive frame for intrinsic Barrier coverage
- Any solid Heavy (e.g., a Rocket-Assisted or Aggressive frame) for boss DPS to cover champion types your build doesn't

## Stat Priority
Class > Grenade > Health > Melee > Weapons > Super. Target ~100 Class (faster Rift + energy gain; 101+ adds an overshield on every Rift cast), push Grenade to 100+ since the Threadling Nest IS your grenade, ~70 Health for GM survivability, ~70 Melee to keep Necrotic poison procs flowing.

## Artifact — Implement of Curiosity *(re-evaluated)*

It is the only artifact that carries the build's entire Threadling/Tangle/Unravel toolkit in one place, and the only one with a Threadling-DAMAGE scaler. This build's output is Threadlings (Weaver's Call, the persistent Threadling Nest, Thread of Rebirth) finishing Unravel/Necrotic-poisoned packs, with Tangles thrown off constantly by Strand kills. Implement provides Horde Shuttle (T1, extra Threadlings off Unravel), Pack Tactics (T1, multiplies Threadling damage AND grants Unstoppable stun at max), Refresh Threads (T1, recycles Strand ability energy), Threaded Blast (T2) and Tangled Web (T3) to turn the Tangle stream into AoE + Suspend lockdown, and Elemental Overdrive (T3) to reward the mono-Strand loadout. No other artifact stacks these: Tablet of Ruin and Queensfoil Censer each have only ONE Threadling generator (Horde Shuttle, at T2/T3) and zero Threadling-damage scaling — they're tuned for Void volatile / Arc bolt-charge / Solar radiant. NPA Repulsion Regulator has Strand pieces but amplifies weapon damage on debuffed targets rather than manufacturing and scaling Threadlings, which is where this build's damage actually comes from. Mechanic coverage, not element-matching, decides it, and Implement wins outright.

## Gameplay Loop
1) Open by casting Rift (Weaver's Call) to weave Threadlings and release any perched ones. 2) Hold the grenade input to deploy the Threadling Nest (Mindspun Invocation) on a chokepoint or boss — it acts as a turret, continuously spawning Threadlings. 3) Wade in with Arcane Needle melee to apply Unravel + Necrotic poison; defeated poisoned enemies spread poison to the pack. 4) Threadlings (buffed by Thread of Evolution) finish the room while Impact Induction/Thread of Generation refund grenade energy to keep the Nest and your next Threadling Grenade online. 5) Pick up Orbs for Woven Mail (Thread of Warding) and grenade energy. 6) Re-deploy the Nest as it expires and re-cast Rift on cooldown to keep Threadling uptime saturated. 7) Pop Needlestorm to reset a room, flood extra Threadlings, or add a burst to a boss. The loop is self-sustaining: kills -> energy -> Nest/grenade -> more kills.

## Strengths
Best-in-class sustained add-clear via the new persistent Threadling Nest turret plus chaining Necrotic poison and Unravel; near-permanent grenade uptime through Impact Induction + Thread of Generation; strong survivability (Healing Rift + Woven Mail from Thread of Warding) for Master/GM; low ammo dependence since Threadlings and poison do the work; the reworked Mindspun Invocation makes this far stronger than its pre-9.7.0 version, and Service of Luzaku now directly synergizes.

## Weaknesses
Mediocre raw single-target boss DPS — Threadlings and poison are AoE-oriented, so a dedicated Heavy is needed for DPS phases. Threadling pathing can be unreliable against airborne/elevated targets. Necrotic Grip requires getting into melee range, which is risky against snipers/Champions in GMs. Output ramps over a few seconds, so it is weaker in burst-it-down scenarios than damage-buff supers. Relies on a steady stream of adds to fuel the poison/Threadling snowball.
