## Exotic — Skull of Dire Ahamkara (helmet)
In Monument of Triumph it was reworked to enable the double Nova Bomb: after your initial Nova Bomb cast you get a short window to instantly cast a second Nova Bomb (Nova Bomb: Lance). Destroying a Cataclysm Nova Bomb with the follow-up Lance spawns a Nova Vortex alongside the tracking seekers, dramatically increasing Super damage and add-clear. It also retains its damage-resistance-while-supering identity.

## Abilities
- **Super:** Nova Bomb: Cataclysm — slow, seeking, splits into tracking seekers. With Skull of Dire Ahamkara you get a brief window after the cast to fire a second Nova Bomb (Nova Bomb: Lance / "Nova Lance"); landing the Lance into the Cataclysm detonation spawns a Nova Vortex alongside the seekers ("double Nova Bomb"). Cataclysm is chosen over Vortex because its kill count refunds the most Super energy through the loop.
- **Class Ability:** Healing Rift
- **Grenade:** Vortex Grenade (lingering Void damage feeds Echo of Provision melee returns, Devour procs, and Volatile via Echo of Instability)
- **Melee:** Soul Siphon (new Voidwalker melee/aspect ability) — land a powered melee to ready it, press melee again to drain up to ~4 enemies in front of you: deals Void damage, grants Void Overshield, and returns Class-ability energy. Damage scales with the Melee stat past 100.

### Aspects
- Soul Siphon (new Void aspect — the readied two-step drain melee that fuels overshield + class-ability energy)
- Feed the Void (every Void ability kill procs Devour: full heal + grenade energy on kill — the engine that keeps the loop alive)

### Fragments
- Echo of Provision — damaging enemies with a grenade returns Melee energy (backbone of the loop; turns one Vortex grenade into your next Soul Siphon charge without needing kills)
- Echo of Harvest — Void-Weakened precision kills create Orbs of Power and a Void Breach (feeds armor mods and Devour)
- Echo of Instability — grenade kills grant Volatile Rounds (now +damage vs champions in 9.7.0)
- Echo of Starvation — picking up an Orb or Void Breach grants/extends Devour (insurance for the Devour uptime)
- Echo of Undermining — Void grenades Weaken targets (sets up Echo of Harvest + boss DPS, since Weaken is now a flat +15% all-damage debuff)

## Weapons
- Bad Juju (Exotic Pulse) — String of Curses stacks on every kill for Super energy and sustains Devour; near-mandatory for the loop's Super uptime
- Graviton Lance (Exotic Pulse) alt — Void add-clear chains that refresh Devour and spread Volatile without Bad Juju
- A Void Heavy GL or Rocket (Aggressive/Adaptive frame) for boss DPS while the Weaken debuff is up — champion-agnostic now since anti-champion is intrinsic by frame
- Any Void legendary primary with Destabilizing Rounds (spreads Volatile, feeds Echo of Harvest Orbs/Breaches)
- Heavy machine gun (Aggressive-frame legendary) for Unstoppable coverage and sustained boss damage — Xenophage only as an alternative if you drop Bad Juju (one exotic weapon at a time)

## Stat Priority
New 6-stat Armor 3.0 system (0–200, tiers gone): Melee FIRST (push 150–180+ — Soul Siphon damage and your whole melee→class loop scale with it past 100) → Super (~100, double Nova uptime) → Class (100+, Healing Rift cadence + overshield-on-cast past 100) → Health (~70+) → Grenade (~70) → Weapons. Snapshot target: ~180 Melee / 100 Super / 100+ Class.

## Artifact — NPA Repulsion Regulator *(re-evaluated)*

The doc's Queensfoil Censer pick is wrong on the merits: it's a Solar/Stasis/Rocket artifact, and three of its four "starred" perks are misread. Revitalizing Blast is actually "stun a Champion -> ignite + clear shield stun" (not the ability/healing refund the doc claims), To Shreds is a Strand sever->unravel/Woven Mail perk (this build runs zero Strand), and Anti-Champion Nosecone/Argent Ordnance only matter if you carry a Rocket Launcher — the ACTUAL equipped loadout (Monte Carlo / Epicurean Void Fusion / Retrofit Escapade Void MG) has no rocket. So Queensfoil contributes almost nothing to the real loop. NPA Repulsion Regulator is the correct artifact because it is a pure Void/Volatile/Weaken/Void-Breach engine that maps 1:1 onto this build's identity: Feed the Void Devour, Echo of Undermining Weaken, Echo of Instability Volatile, Echo of Harvest Breaches, Soul Siphon Void Overshield. Every kill is on a Void-debuffed target, which is exactly what NPA's Unto the Breach, Protective Breach, Supernova, and Void Weapon Channeling want. It turns the existing sustain loop into a Breach->Overshield->Weaken-pulse flywheel and amps the Void-weapon boss window — not by element-matching, but because the perk triggers ARE the build's mechanics.

## Gameplay Loop
1) Open with Soul Siphon: land powered melee to ready it, press melee again to drain up to 4 enemies — this grants Void Overshield (survivability) and returns Class-ability energy. 2) Throw Vortex Grenade into the group: Echo of Provision refunds your Melee energy, Echo of Undermining Weakens, Echo of Instability gives Volatile Rounds, and grenade kills proc Feed the Void Devour (full heal + grenade energy). 3) Clean up Weakened targets with Bad Juju / Destabilizing primary — precision kills make Orbs + Void Breaches (Echo of Harvest), which re-up Devour via Echo of Starvation and refill abilities through mods. 4) Class-item Outreach + arm Momentum Transfer keep Soul Siphon recharging off grenade damage and class casts, so you cycle melee→grenade→melee endlessly with permanent Devour and rolling overshield. 5) Bad Juju's String of Curses and your kills feed Super fast; on a boss, cast Nova Bomb: Cataclysm then immediately fire the Skull-enabled Nova Lance into it for the double-detonation Nova Vortex while Echo of Undermining holds the Weaken debuff and To Shreds stacks bonus damage taken for max damage.

## Strengths
Outstanding sustain — permanent Devour plus rolling Void Overshield from Soul Siphon makes it extremely hard to kill in GMs and solo content. Self-sufficient ability economy: the melee→grenade→class loop refunds itself without needing kills (Echo of Provision), so abilities are always available. Huge burst on demand from the double Nova Bomb for bosses/Champions, and strong add-clear via Volatile/Vortex/seekers. Anti-champion is now intrinsic to weapon frames, so you stay free to optimize purely for the loop. Flexible across raids, dungeons, Master/GM Nightfalls, and Onslaught.

## Weaknesses
Boss DPS outside the double-Nova window is mediocre — it leans on weapons and the Weaken debuff rather than a sustained Super, so it is more of a survivability/add-clear king than a top parsing DPS build. Heavily reliant on Bad Juju (or a strong Void primary) for Super uptime; without it the double-Nova cadence drops off. The 9.7.0 active-cooldown cap nerf (190%→125%) means you can't over-stack cooldown mods, so the loop depends on the Melee stat being very high. Soul Siphon's two-step input has a small skill/timing cost, and the Nova Lance follow-up window is tight under pressure.
