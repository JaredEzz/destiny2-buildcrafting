## Exotic — Osmiomancy Gloves (Gauntlets)
Grants a second Coldsnap Grenade charge and improves Coldsnap tracking; freezing a target with Coldsnap returns a large chunk of grenade energy. Net effect: near-permanent Bleak Watcher turret uptime — you can throw a turret AND still have a Coldsnap in the chamber, keeping two-to-three freeze sources active at once. This is the most reliable, highest-uptime endgame Shadebinder exotic. (Alternative: Rime-Coat Raiment — its Bleak Domain perk supercharges a single turret with respawning Stasis crystals + an ice-storm freeze field, better for a defensive bunker/CC style and arguably stronger raw control, but Osmiomancy wins on grenade economy and flexibility for GMs.)

## Abilities
- **Super:** Winter's Wrath (Stasis staff — Penumbral Blast freeze + Shatter slam). Cast for instant room-control freeze, then shatter the frozen group for burst clear; otherwise hold as a panic/clutch CC button in GMs.
- **Class Ability:** Healing Rift — your sustain anchor while you hold a lane behind the turret. (Note: this build's two aspect slots go to Bleak Watcher + Glacial Harvest, so the Frostpulse freeze-on-cast Rift is NOT available; your on-demand Frost Armor comes from Glacial Harvest shard pickups instead. Alternate loadout: swap Glacial Harvest for the Frostpulse aspect if you want a panic-freeze Rift — in 9.7.0 it grants 5 stacks of Frost Armor when it freezes on cast — at the cost of the shard economy.)
- **Grenade:** Coldsnap Grenade (tracking freeze seeker; converted into a Bleak Watcher turret via hold-grenade). Osmiomancy gives a 2nd Coldsnap charge and refunds grenade energy on freeze.
- **Melee:** Penumbral Blast (Stasis ranged melee — freezes the target). Mostly used to proc Frost Armor fragments and freeze a priority/Champion target on demand.

### Aspects
- Bleak Watcher (turn grenade into a Stasis turret that applies x40 Slow / freezes — your engine of constant AoE freeze)
- Glacial Harvest (freezing targets spawns Stasis Shards near them; Shard pickups grant Frost Armor — fuels the 9.7.0 Frost Armor ability economy)

### Fragments
Bleak Watcher + Glacial Harvest grant 4 fragment slots:
- Whisper of Rime (Frost Armor stacks/duration extended — now 6s per stack; core defensive layer and grenade-energy engine)
- Whisper of Chains (damage resistance while near frozen targets or Stasis crystals)
- Whisper of Fissures (increased shatter/Stasis-crystal damage for burst clear and DPS)
- Whisper of Shards (shattering a Stasis crystal boosts grenade recharge — feeds the turret loop)

## Weapons
- Primary: any Stasis Submachine Gun or Auto with Headstone (precision final blows spawn a Stasis crystal) to keep crystals spawning for the shatter loop (e.g., a Stasis SMG for shatter synergy)
- Special: Conditional Finality (exotic Stasis shotgun — freezes on the Stasis barrel; instant single-target/Champion freeze + burst)
- Special alt: a Stasis Glaive or Fusion for add control
- Heavy: Stasis-frame or high-DPS heavy. For boss DPS pair shatter burst with a Heavy GL/LMG; an Unstoppable-frame heavy (Aggressive/High-Impact) now intrinsically stuns Unstops under Anti-Champion 2.0
- Note: under Anti-Champion 2.0 you no longer need an artifact mod — bring weapon frames matching the Champions present (Precision/Adaptive = Barrier, Lightweight/Rapid-Fire = Overload, Aggressive/High-Impact/Heavy/Rocket = Unstoppable)

## Stat Priority
Class (Recovery — push to 100+ for the new overshield-on-rift effect and fast Healing Rift uptime) > Grenade (~70-100 for Coldsnap/Bleak Watcher cooldown — this is your damage/control source) > Health (toward 100 for survivability and Orb HP) > Super (Winter's Wrath uptime) > Melee (~30, only for Penumbral Blast procs) > Weapons (lowest). Target a Demolitionist (Grenade/Class) or Reaver (Class/Melee) armor archetype; Tier 5 armor now drops at max stats so no re-grind.

## Artifact — Slayer Baron's Apothecary Satchel *(re-evaluated)*

Correct pick, and not for element-matching reasons — it is the only artifact in the pool whose perks physically close the Bleak Watcher freeze-shatter-shard loop. Wind Chill turns every Stasis weapon burst into Frost Armor + shards; Crystalline Converter converts the Glacial Harvest shard flood into on-demand crystal generation off the Penumbral Blast melee; Served Cold converts that same shard flood into class-ability energy (Healing Rift); Hail The Storm and Brain Freeze multiply shatter damage and extend the Slow field. No other artifact (NPA/Void, Tablet/Strand-Arc, Encrypted/Kinetic-Arc, Implement/Solar-Strand) interacts with shards, crystals, freeze, or Frost Armor — they would leave half the kit (Glacial Harvest, Whisper of Rime/Shards/Fissures) doing nothing. This artifact is the engine block this subclass bolts onto.

## Gameplay Loop
Open by casting Healing Rift as your anchor, then immediately hold-grenade to drop a Bleak Watcher turret on the densest add lane — it perpetually applies Slow and freezes. Throw your (second, via Osmiomancy) Coldsnap to freeze a second cluster; Osmiomancy refunds grenade energy on the freeze so you almost always have a turret or Coldsnap ready. Frozen enemies spawn Stasis Shards (Glacial Harvest) — sweep them up to stack and refresh Frost Armor (now a grenade-energy engine in 9.7.0) and trigger Wind Chill/Crystalline Converter from the artifact. Shoot Stasis crystals / frozen targets with your primary to shatter for AoE clear (Whisper of Fissures boosts the shatter damage, and Whisper of Shards refunds grenade energy on shatter, closing the loop). Use Penumbral Blast melee or Conditional Finality to lock a Champion in place — then apply the matching intrinsic Anti-Champion 2.0 weapon frame (no artifact mod needed). Save Winter's Wrath for emergency room-wide freeze or a shatter-burst on a mini-boss. Sustain comes from layered Frost Armor (35% DR at 5 stacks, ~50% via Whisper of Rime extensions) + Whisper of Chains near crystals, keeping you near-immortal while everything on screen is frozen.

## Strengths
Best-in-class crowd control in the game — permanent AoE freeze/Slow lockdown trivializes add-dense GMs, Master Lost Sectors, Onslaught, and raid encounters. Extremely high survivability via the reworked Frost Armor (now both heavy DR AND a grenade-energy economy), Whisper of Chains, and Healing Rift. Near-zero downtime on Bleak Watcher thanks to Osmiomancy. Champions/elites are simply frozen and ignored. Forgiving and safe — you control space rather than trade DPS.

## Weaknesses
Mediocre single-target boss DPS compared to Solar/Void DPS supers — shatter is great for adds, weak on bosses, so you lean on your Heavy weapon and team for damage phases. The slower 9.7.0 ability-cooldown sandbox (active cooldown cap cut 190%→125%) means you can't brute-force turret spam without the full Grenade Kickstart/Frost Armor economy in place. Some boss arenas resist or are immune to freeze, blunting the core mechanic. Less burst-flashy than the meta double-Nova Voidwalker, so it's a control pick, not a damage pick.
