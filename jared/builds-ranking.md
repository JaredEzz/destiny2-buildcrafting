# Build Rankings — Filling the 20 In-Game Loadout Slots

> All 20 builds graded 1–10 on six criteria. **Overall = straight average**. The in-game grid is **4×5 = 20 slots** — every build fits.

## Rubric
1. **Add-Clear** — sustained room clearing without ammo anxiety
2. **Boss DPS** — burst + sustained damage contribution
3. **Survivability** — how hard you are to kill in GM-tier content
4. **Loop Smoothness** — how self-sustaining the rotation is (no fiddly timing or babysitting)
5. **Team Value** — heals/buffs/debuffs/CC the fireteam feels
6. **Fun** — power fantasy + novelty
